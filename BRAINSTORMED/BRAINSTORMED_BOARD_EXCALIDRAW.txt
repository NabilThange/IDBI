
the Problem Statement:
<p>Problem Statement 1: Digital Wealth Management</p>
<p>Wealth management and advisory services remain fragmented and largely inaccessible to large number of customers. Absence of comprehensive, customer investment behaviour and spending habits limits the ability to provide timely, personalized, data-driven guidance.</p>
<p>Expected Outcome
Bank is looking for AI powered Digital Wealth management (Avatar Base) Application which integrates into the bank’s mobile application, to deliver personalized and scalable wealth advisory services through an intuitive digital interface.</p>



<p>COMPETITOR'S STUFFS : e (ICICI&#39;s iPal, HDFC&#39;s Eva</p>

<p>What a human wealth advisor actually does</p>
<p>Strip away the suits and the office, and a good advisor does four things:</p>
<ol>
<li>Translates — turns &quot;asset allocation&quot; into &quot;here&#39;s what happens to your money&quot;</li>
<li>Listens for context a spreadsheet can&#39;t see — you mention a wedding coming up, a parent&#39;s medical bills, anxiety about job security — and adjusts advice accordingly</li>
<li>Reassures under pressure — when markets drop 15%, their real job is talking you out of panic-selling</li>
<li>Is accountable — a name and a face you can be annoyed at if the advice was bad</li>
</ol>

Understanding problem:

<p>Current Scenario</p>
<p>Imagine a customer opens the banking app.</p>
<p>Current experience:</p>
<p>• Checks balance
• Transfers money
• Pays bills</p>
<p>That&#39;s all.</p>
<p>The bank has valuable data like:</p>
<p>• Salary credits
• Monthly expenses
• Savings
• Spending categories
• Existing deposits
• Loan EMIs</p>
<p>But this data isn&#39;t actively used to provide financial advice.</p>

<p>Desired Solution</p>
<p>The bank wants an AI Wealth Advisor integrated into its mobile app.</p>
<p>Example conversation:</p>
<p>Customer</p>
<p>I save around ₹20,000 every month. Where should I invest?</p>
<p>AI</p>
<p>Based on your income, spending habits, and moderate risk appetite:
• &quot;₹8,000 → SIP in Equity Mutual Funds&quot;
• &quot;₹5,000 → Emergency Fund&quot;
• &quot;₹4,000 → Gold ETF&quot;
• &quot;₹3,000 → Fixed Deposit&quot;</p>

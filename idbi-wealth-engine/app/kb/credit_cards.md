# IDBI Bank Credit Cards

## Credit Card Portfolio

### 1. IDBI Premier Credit Card

**Annual Fee**: ₹999 (reversed on spending ₹1 lakh annually)

**Welcome Benefits**
- 2,000 reward points on card activation

**Rewards**
- 2 reward points per ₹100 spent (except fuel)
- 10 reward points per ₹100 on travel bookings
- 5 reward points per ₹100 on dining
- 1 reward point = ₹0.25

**Benefits**
- Complimentary lounge access: 4 times per year
- Fuel surcharge waiver: 1% (₹250 per transaction)
- Dining discounts: 15% at partner restaurants
- Movie ticket offers

**Eligibility**
- Minimum income: ₹3 lakh per annum
- Age: 21-65 years

### 2. IDBI Titanium Credit Card

**Annual Fee**: ₹499 (reversed on spending ₹50,000 annually)

**Rewards**
- 1 reward point per ₹100 spent
- 5 reward points per ₹100 on groceries
- Bonus 500 points on birthday month

**Benefits**
- Complimentary lounge access: 2 times per year
- Fuel surcharge waiver: 1%
- EMI conversion facility
- Lost card liability cover

**Eligibility**
- Minimum income: ₹2 lakh per annum
- Age: 21-65 years

### 3. IDBI Cashback Credit Card

**Annual Fee**: ₹750 (reversed on spending ₹75,000 annually)

**Cashback**
- 5% cashback on utility bills (max ₹500/month)
- 2% cashback on online shopping
- 1% cashback on all other spends
- Maximum cashback: ₹5,000 per year

**Benefits**
- No cashback redemption required (auto-credited)
- Zero liability on lost card
- Purchase protection
- EMI facility on high-value purchases

**Eligibility**
- Minimum income: ₹2.5 lakh per annum
- Age: 21-60 years

## Interest Rates
- Retail purchases: 3.50% per month (42% p.a.)
- Cash advance: 3.75% per month (45% p.a.)

## Credit Limits
- Premier Card: ₹50,000 to ₹10 lakh
- Titanium Card: ₹25,000 to ₹5 lakh
- Cashback Card: ₹30,000 to ₹7 lakh

## Fees and Charges
- Cash advance fee: 2.5% (min ₹250)
- Over-limit fee: ₹500
- Late payment fee: ₹500-₹1,200
- Card replacement: ₹100
- Rent payment fee: 1% + GST

## Payment Due Date
- 45 days interest-free period
- Minimum payment: 5% of outstanding or ₹500

## Balance Transfer
- Transfer balances from other bank cards
- Special rates: 0.99% per month for 6 months
- Processing fee: 2% of transfer amount

## EMI Conversion
- Convert purchases above ₹2,500 to EMI
- Tenure: 3, 6, 9, 12 months
- Interest rate: 13-15% p.a.

## Documents Required
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statement
- Address proof

##### Treasury - Currency Exchange

IDBI Bank also buys / sells Foreign Currency notes at our selected branches (all Trade Finance branches and selected ‘C’ category Branches), with respect to the following currencies:

* USD - US Dollar
* GBP - Great Britain Pound
* EUR - Euro
* JPY - Japanese Yen
* HKD - Hong Kong Dollar
* AUD - Australian Dollar
* CAD - Canadian Dollar
* CHF - Swiss Francs
* SGD – Singapore Dollar
* AED – U.A.E Dirham

  

Existing customers of the Bank can also encash Travellers Cheque (TC) denominated in any of the above mentioned currencies at our selected branches (all Trade Finance branches and selected ‘C’ category Branches).

# IDBI Bank Personal Loans

## Overview
IDBI Bank offers personal loans for various needs including medical emergencies, weddings, education, travel, and debt consolidation. Quick approval with minimal documentation.

## Interest Rates
- Starting from: 10.50% p.a.
- Up to: 16.00% p.a.
- Rate based on: Credit score, income, loan amount, and tenure

## Loan Amount
- Minimum: ₹50,000
- Maximum: ₹20 lakh
- Based on income and eligibility

## Tenure
- Minimum: 12 months
- Maximum: 60 months (5 years)

## Eligibility

### Salaried Employees
- Age: 21 to 60 years
- Minimum Income: ₹20,000 per month
- Work Experience: Minimum 1 year
- Current Employment: Minimum 6 months

### Self-Employed Professionals
- Age: 25 to 65 years
- Minimum Income: ₹2.5 lakh per annum
- Business Vintage: Minimum 2 years
- ITR: Last 2 years

## Processing Fee
- 2% of loan amount + GST
- Minimum: ₹1,000
- Maximum: ₹10,000

## Features
1. **Quick disbursal**: Within 24-48 hours
2. **Minimal documentation**: Simple KYC process
3. **Flexible tenure**: Choose 1-5 years repayment
4. **No collateral**: Unsecured loan
5. **Prepayment allowed**: Partial/full prepayment with minimal charges

## Documents Required
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Last 3 months salary slips (salaried)
- Last 6 months bank statement
- Address proof
- Photograph

## EMI Calculation Example
Loan Amount: ₹5,00,000

Total Repayment: ₹5,97,852

## Use Cases
- Medical emergencies
- Wedding expenses
- Debt consolidation
- Home renovation
- Travel/vacation
- Education expenses
- Electronic purchases

## Prepayment Charges
- Within first 6 months: 4% of outstanding principal
- After 6 months: 2% of outstanding principal
- After 1 year: No charges

## Application Process
1. Fill online application
2. Submit documents
3. Credit verification (CIBIL check)
4. Loan approval
5. E-sign agreement
6. Disbursement to bank account

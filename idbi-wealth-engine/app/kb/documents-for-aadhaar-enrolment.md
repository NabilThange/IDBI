                          What if someone does not have necessary documentary proof?

                                •     If the Head of Household has the required documents, a ration card or
                                      any other document that establishes relationship with, and has the
                                      same address as the head of household will do.

                                •     Even if you have no documents, you can still enroll with the help of an
                                      introducer. Introducer can be the one who has original Aadhaar
                                      number issued by UIDAI

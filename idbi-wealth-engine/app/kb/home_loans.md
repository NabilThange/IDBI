# IDBI Bank Home Loans

## Overview
IDBI Bank offers home loans for purchase, construction, renovation, and extension of residential properties at competitive interest rates.

## Interest Rates (As of July 2026)

### Regular Home Loans
- Loan Amount up to ₹30 lakh: 8.50% p.a. onwards
- Loan Amount ₹30 lakh to ₹75 lakh: 8.40% p.a. onwards
- Loan Amount above ₹75 lakh: 8.30% p.a. onwards

### Special Rates for Women Borrowers
Women borrowers get 0.05% concession on applicable interest rates.

### Special Rates for Salaried Employees
IDBI employees and employees of select corporate clients: 8.25% p.a. onwards

## Loan Amount
- Minimum: ₹1 lakh
- Maximum: Up to ₹5 crore (subject to eligibility)
- LTV Ratio: Up to 90% of property value (80% for loans above ₹75 lakh)

## Repayment Tenure
- Minimum: 1 year
- Maximum: 30 years
- Tenure based on age (max age at maturity: 70 years)

## Eligibility Criteria

### Salaried Individuals
- Age: 21 to 65 years
- Minimum Income: ₹25,000 per month
- Work Experience: Minimum 2 years (1 year in current organization)
- Credit Score: Minimum 700

### Self-Employed Individuals
- Age: 21 to 65 years
- Minimum Income: ₹3 lakh per annum
- Business Vintage: Minimum 3 years
- ITR: Last 2 years mandatory

## Processing Fee
- Up to ₹30 lakh: 0.50% of loan amount (min ₹2,500)
- Above ₹30 lakh: 0.35% of loan amount
- GST extra as applicable

## Features and Benefits
1. **No prepayment charges**: For floating rate home loans
2. **Overdraft facility**: Withdrawal facility on home loan account
3. **Attractive interest rates**: Starting from 8.30% p.a.
4. **Balance transfer**: Transfer existing home loan with top-up
5. **Quick disbursement**: Fast processing and disbursement
6. **Flexible EMI**: Step-up, step-down EMI options available

## Types of Home Loans

### 1. Home Purchase Loan
- For buying ready-to-move-in properties
- LTV: Up to 90%
- Tenure: Up to 30 years

### 2. Home Construction Loan
- For construction on owned land
- Disbursement in stages based on construction progress
- LTV: Up to 80% of construction cost

### 3. Home Extension Loan
- For extending existing house
- Minimum loan: ₹1 lakh
- Tenure: Up to 20 years

### 4. Home Improvement Loan
- For renovation and repair
- Minimum loan: ₹1 lakh
- Maximum: ₹25 lakh
- Tenure: Up to 15 years

### 5. Plot Loan
- For purchasing residential plot
- LTV: Up to 75%
- Maximum tenure: 15 years

### 6. Balance Transfer
- Transfer existing home loan
- Additional top-up loan available
- Lower interest rate benefits

## Tax Benefits
Under Income Tax Act, 1961:
- **Section 80C**: Principal repayment deduction up to ₹1.5 lakh
- **Section 24(b)**: Interest deduction up to ₹2 lakh per year
- **Section 80EEA**: Additional ₹1.5 lakh for first-time home buyers (property value up to ₹45 lakh)

## Documents Required

### Identity Proof
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport/Voter ID/Driving License

### Address Proof
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport/Voter ID/Utility bills

### Income Proof (Salaried)
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Form 16 for last 2 years

### Income Proof (Self-Employed)
- ITR for last 2 years
- Audited financials
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Business proof documents

### Property Documents
- Sale agreement/construction agreement
- Approved building plan
- Property tax receipts
- NOC from society/builder

## Application Process
1. Check eligibility and calculate EMI
2. Fill online application form
3. Submit documents
4. Credit appraisal and property valuation
5. Loan sanction
6. Legal verification of property
7. Loan disbursement

## Important Terms
- **EMI**: Equated Monthly Installment
- **LTV**: Loan-to-Value ratio
- **CIBIL**: Credit score (minimum 700 recommended)
- **FOIR**: Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio (max 50-60%)

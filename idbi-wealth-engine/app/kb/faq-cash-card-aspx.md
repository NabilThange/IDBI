##### Cash card queries - IDBI Bank Cash card FAQ's

What is a Cash Card?

* Cash Card is a Rupee denominated, multi utility reloadable pre-paid Card.
* Valid for a period of 3 years from the date of issuance (marked on the face of the card).

Where can I use this Cash Card?

* Cash Card can be used to make purchases at over 10 lakh establishments in India. It can also be used to withdraw cash at any ATMs (including IDBI Bank 3700 ATMs) in India.
* Can be used more than once. The card can be used to make repeated purchases / withdrawals till the balance available on the card.

What is the loading limit?

* The maximum loading limit is Rs.2, 00,000/- provided balance should not exceed Rs.2, 00,000/- at any point of time.

What are the main Benefits of this Card to a Customer?

* No Account required. Need not be a customer of the bank
* Can be used multiple times for cash withdrawals and purchase transactions
* Reloadable as and when required with the maximum limit of Rs.2, 00,000/- provided balance should not exceed Rs.2, 00,000/- at any point of time.
* Keeps a check on your spending.

What are the main Benefits of this Card to a Corporate?

* Hassle free alternative for
  1. Reimbursements to employees
  2. Salary disbursements
  3. Incentive payment to employees / staff.
* No Account Required. Need not be a customer of the bank. KYC norms needs to be fulfilled.
* Convenient alternative to demand drafts, cheques or cash disbursements to employees/staff.
* No reconciliation problems
* Reloadable which means you can disburse more cash to the same employee/staff as and when required up to Rs. 2, 00,000/-, as per the requirement of the corporate.

What are the charges for this card?

* Issuance fee = Rs.140 + GST
* Reloading Fee = Rs.10+ GST per instance
* Refund Fee = Rs.50 + GST
* There is no charge for transactions at IDBI Bank ATMs but Rs.21/- + GST per transaction will be charged for all cash withdrawal transactions and Rs. 9/- + GST for all Balance Inquiry transactions on any other Bank ATMs in India.
* There is no charge if the card is used at a Merchant Establishment for purchases. However, for use at Petrol Pumps and at Railways, a surcharge of 2.5% + GST Or Rs.11.80/- whichever is higher shall be applicable as per industry standards.

What will the Cash Card pack contain?

The Cash Card pack will contain the following:

* Welcome letter along with Cash Card. Welcome letter has a Cash Card Reference Number and Customer ID (this number is to be quoted for claiming unutilized balances).
* ATM PIN
* Terms & Conditions

What if my Cash Card is lost or stolen?

Original Purchaser or the Recipient of the Cash Card may report a card as lost. All you need is to call the IDBI Bank's Phone Banking Centre 1800-22-6999 and quote the Cash card reference number mentioned on the card mailer. The card will be deactivated immediately to prevent misuse.

How can I claim the unutilized balance remaining on this card after expiry of the card?

The original purchaser/ Cash card beneficiary can fill out a form asking for a refund of the unutilized amount on the card, post expiry of the card.

What are daily cash withdrawal and purchase limit on the Cards?

* ATM withdrawal - Rs.50,000/-
* POS – Rs.25,000/-
* ECOM- Rs.25,000-/
* Cash at POS – Rs.2,000/-
* Daily Contact Less – Rs.10,000/-

# Credit Card Reward Program - IDBI Delight

# Overview

IDBI Delight is IDBI Bank’s Credit Card rewards program. It is an IDBI Bank endeavor to strengthen our bond of friendship by adding joy, excitement & value to the banking experience of our Credit Card customers.

Being a part of this program, you will earn Delight Points each time you spend on in-store buys, online purchases or utility bill payments, etc., using your IDBI Bank Credit Card. These earned Delight Points can be spent on over 7000+ exciting rewards spread across various categories:

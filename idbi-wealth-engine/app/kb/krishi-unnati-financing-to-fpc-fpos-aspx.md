# Financing to Farmer Producer Companies / FPOs

[Apply Now](apply-now.aspx)

# Overview

The program is aimed at providing loan support to Farmer Producer Collectives (FPC) & Farmer
Producer Organizations (FPO) of small and marginal farmers. FPC/FPO are formed to organize
small & marginal farmers for (a) backward linkage for inputs like seeds, fertilizers, credit,
insurance and extension services and (b) forward linkages such as collective marketing,
processing, market -led agriculture production, etc. to gain collective bargaining power.

# Who is eligible for the Loan?

Farmer Producer Company (FPC) & Farmer Producer Organizations (FPO) - Registered societies & Trusts.

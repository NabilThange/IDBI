# IDBI Bank Loan for Land Development

[Apply Now](apply-now.aspx)

##### Overview

Land Development aims to convert fallow/barren or uncultivable land into cultivable. This includes
land leveling, land reclamation etc. Individual farmer with good repayment record is the
eligibility criteria. Loan quantum will be considered on the basis of engineers estimate
engineers certificate as per government-scheduled rate.

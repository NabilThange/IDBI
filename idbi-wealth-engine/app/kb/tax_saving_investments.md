# Tax Saving Investment Options - IDBI Bank

## Section 80C Investments (Deduction up to ₹1.5 lakh)

### 1. ELSS Mutual Funds
- Tax deduction: Up to ₹1.5 lakh
- Lock-in period: 3 years (shortest among 80C options)
- Expected returns: 12-15% p.a.
- Minimum investment: ₹500 per month via SIP

### 2. Public Provident Fund (PPF)
- Tax deduction: Up to ₹1.5 lakh
- Lock-in period: 15 years
- Interest rate: 7.1% p.a. (govt. backed)
- Minimum: ₹500, Maximum: ₹1.5 lakh per year
- Tax-free interest and maturity amount

### 3. Tax Saving Fixed Deposit
- Lock-in: 5 years
- Interest rate: 7.75% p.a. (8.25% for senior citizens)
- Minimum: ₹10,000
- Premature withdrawal: Not allowed
- Interest taxable as per slab

### 4. National Savings Certificate (NSC)
- Tenure: 5 years
- Interest rate: 7.7% p.a.
- Minimum: ₹1,000
- Interest taxable but re-investment qualifies for 80C

## Section 80D - Health Insurance
- Self & Family: ₹25,000 (₹50,000 for senior citizens)
- Parents: Additional ₹25,000 (₹50,000 if senior citizens)
- Preventive health checkup: ₹5,000
- Total max deduction: ₹1 lakh

## Section 80CCD(1B) - NPS
- Additional deduction: ₹50,000 (over and above 80C limit)
- Lock-in: Till 60 years
- Market-linked returns
- Partial tax-free withdrawal at maturity

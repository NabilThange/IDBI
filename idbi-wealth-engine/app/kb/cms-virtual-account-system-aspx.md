##### Virtual Account System

VAS is an e – Collection products which facilitates Clients for structured collection of funds in online mode from its Dealer / Vendors.

##### Brief Features:

* Automatic instantaneous credit of e-remittances to customers accounts through virtual accounts

* Instant identification of the remitter with complete details of amount, remitter’s name and UTR Number

* On line view of remittances through net banking

* Email alerts to remitter and beneficiary

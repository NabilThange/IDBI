# Overview

IDBI Loan for Services Sector (ILSS) can be extended to all kind of business enterprises, self-employed and professionals,
engaged in providing or rendering service.

Some examples of the service sector enterprises and professionals are  
a. **Professionals such as:** Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Engineers, Architects, Surveyors, Interior designers, Management Consultants etc.  
b. **Self-employed/service enterprises such as:** Contractors, Travel Agents & Tour Operators, Clearing & forwarding agents, Beauty parlors etc.

##### The details of the product are mentioned below:

###### Eligible Segments

All kind of business enterprises, self-employed and professionals, engaged in providing or rendering service.

###### Facility

Term loan & Overdraft/ Cash Credit

###### Purpose

OD/CC: To meet Working Capital requirements  
Term Loan: For Purchase of business premises, equipment, computers, furnitures, undertaking development/expansion/additions/repairs and renovation of business premises and infrastructure.

###### Loan Amount

* Minimum: Above Rs. 10 lakh
* Maximum: Rs. 500 lakh

###### Tenor

Term Loan: Max 7 years  
CC/OD: Max 12 months (Annual renewal)

###### Pricing

Linked to banks Repo Linked Lending Rate (RLLR) and Rating

###### Security

* Term Loan: Exclusive Charge on assets created out of bank finance.
* Working Capital: Hypothecation of stock, book debts and assets created out of bank finance.
* Collateral: Mortgage of residential/commercial properties of appropriate value.

###### Guarantee

<

Personal guarantee of the promoter directors, Partners

###### Margin

Cash Credit : 25% on stock and Book debts  
Overdraft : NIL  
Term loan : 25 %

###### Processing Charges

As per Extant applicable Schedule of Charges.

# VISA Classic PayWave Debit Card

# About The Card

* Simply wave/tap the card on the contactless Point of Sale (PoS) and pay without entering PIN for purchases upto Rs. 5000.
* Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 25,000/- and make purchases worth Rs. 50,000/- in a day at Point of Sale (POS).

##### Features

Daily Limits

Withdrawal limits are Rs. 25,000/- at the ATM and Rs. 50,000/- at POS. Limits are per day/per card and are subject to the available balance in the customer’s account.

International Validity

The Classic Debit Card can be used both in India and abroad to make purchases at Merchant locations and withdraw local currency at ATMs. The customer is debited in INR.
All transactions are reflected in the account statement in INR and merchant location details are captured.

Discounts at merchant establishments

We make tie-ups with different merchants from time to time to offer discounts to our Debit Card holders. Customers can avail of
this discount by making purchases through the Debit Card at these merchant locations.

Online transactions

The Debit Card can be used online, for shopping, booking air/rail/movie tickets & utility bill payments.

Better tracking

Receive instant SMS alerts on transaction made on your Card.

Insurance Cover\*

Customers are covered with an insurance cover upto Rs. 1 lac for lost and counterfeit cards.

*\*Effective 1st July 2013, for Insurance claims to be accepted and processed; there should be a minimum of 2
purchase transactions using the Debit Card in last 3 months prior to the event date.*

Loyalty Program#

Customers will gain one loyalty point for every Rs. 100 spent on the Debit Card.

*#Loyalty point reward structure as well as reward of loyalty points for different merchant*

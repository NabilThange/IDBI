##### The Right To Information Act, 2005

###### **Details in respect of the information available to or held by it, reduced in an electronic form**

The Bank operates in a highly automated environment. All its customer dealings, transactions and outcomes of such transactions are maintained in electronic form. The Bank also holds most of its operational, payment/ receipts information in electronic form. Such information is either a part of the core banking system or residing in separate systems.

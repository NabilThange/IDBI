##### ALERT

×

It has come to IDBI Bank Ltd.’s attention that some unscrupulous persons/ recruitment agencies have been making job offers in the name of the IDBI Bank Ltd. by issuing fake offer letters, using Bank’s name, logo and address in fake stationery. The Bank has not hired the services of any agency or individual to recruit personnel on its behalf or collect any money/ commission/ charges for training etc. IDBI Bank announces its job openings only via notifications in leading newspapers of India and on the official website viz., https://www.idbi.bank.in/idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx. General public is hereby advised to exercise caution while dealing with such unscrupulous persons/ agencies.

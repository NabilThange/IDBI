Second 100 Days Campaign –“Saksham Niveshak”
       for KYC Updates & Related Updations and Shareholder Engagement to Prevent Transfer of
                               Unpaid/Unclaimed Dividends to IEPF

In continuation to the earlier campaign in accordance with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) circular
dated 16th July 2025, IDBI Bank Ltd. has launched a second 100 Days Campaign - “Saksham Niveshak”,
from 1st April 2026 to 9th July 2026 with an expanded scope to further facilitate the direct payment of unclaimed/
unpaid dividends to the rightful shareholders.

This initiative aims to assist shareholders in:

    Proactive Engagement – Reach out to the shareholders to update KYC details, bank mandates, nominee
     information, and contact details.
    Timely dividend processing - Swift processing of unpaid/unclaimed dividends for the Financial
     Years 2022-23, 2023-24 and 2024-25.
    Prevent Unnecessary Transfers – Avoid the transfer of shares to IEPF by ensuring all eligible shareholders
     make necessary claims.
    Direct claim settlement – Enable shareholders to receive their rightful claim directly from the company.

2. IDBI Bank is constantly familiarizing its Directors with the functioning of the Bank
  including essential areas such as Profile of the Bank/ nature of industry, sub committees
  of the Board, their roles, responsibilities, details on delegation of powers, financial and
  other controls and systems, risk, etc. Apart from this, the Independent Directors have
  also been apprised specifically about their roles, responsibilities and functions (in terms
  of various statutory/ regulatory provisions) to be discharged by them as members of the
  Board and various sub-committees of the Board, vide appointment letters issued to them.
  The same are also available on Bank’s website under the following link:
  https://www.idbibank.in/pdf/Letters-of-appointment-of-Indepedent-Director.pdf
Overview of Familiarization Programmes Conducted by the Bank:

(i)     The Bank regularly acquaints the Board about the updates in the provisions of law
        stipulated by RBI/SEBI or any other regulatory authority through a separate paper
        brought in the Board meeting from time to time.
(ii)    The Bank conducts induction programme for its new directors covering areas
        relating to Governance, finance and accounts, compliance, risk, inter audit, human
        resource and operations. They are also briefed on their obligations under the various
        policies/ code and on the terms of reference of the various Committees of the Board
        of Directors of the Bank.
(iii)   The Directors are in regular contact with the Whole Time Directors and Vertical
        Heads of the Bank to get a first-hand account of business and regulatory
        environment and other issues concerning the operations of the Bank.
(iv)    The Bank organises presentations from external agencies for the Directors on the
        recent matters to keep them abreast of latest business environment.
(v)     The whole time Directors of the Bank have been regularly attending the Meetings
        organized by various Institutions. Further, the Bank keeps nominating Independent
        Directors to various programmes on relevant topics for Directors of Banks
        organized by IICA, IOD, ICAI, CAFRAL, ISRBT, ISB, etc. These programmes
        have facilitated the Directors to familiarize themselves with emerging
        developments/challenges facing the Banking sector in general apart from important
        Corporate Governance aspects impacting their role as Directors in particular.

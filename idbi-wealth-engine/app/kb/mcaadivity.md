        Members of the Bank are aware that, the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) has taken “Green Initiative in Corporate

        circular no.17/2011 dated April 21, 2011 and circular no.18/2011 dated April 29, 2011 issued by MCA, Companies can now
        send various notices and documents including Annual Report, to their shareholders through electronic mode to the registered

How do I generate PIN for my IDBI Bank Debit Card?

Go Green! You can now generate PIN for your IDBI Bank debit cum ATM card without visiting the branch or waiting for it to arrive on your address. We've made PIN generation for our customers as convenient as it could get.

You may choose any of the following modes to generate your PIN instantly:

**Please note: Joint account holders using an add-on debit card with mobile number in bank records can generate ATM PIN through IDBI Bank ATM, SMS & Missed Call only.**

**Option 1 Through Phone Banking**

Step 1: Keep your Customer ID and 16-digit Debit cum ATM Card number handy.

Step 2: Dial any of our toll free phone banking numbers 1800-209-4324 or 1800-22-1070 and if overseas call +91-22-67719100(call charges applicable).

Step 3: Select your preferred language and in the main menu choose the option "generate ATM PIN "

Step 4: Follow the instructions of the Interactive Voice Response (IVR) and create a 4-digit ATM PIN of your choice.

Step 5: Use your debit card with the newly created PIN at any ATM or Point of Sale terminal to activate it.

---

**Option 2) Through IDBI Bank ATM**

Step 1: Dip your Debit Card in the card slot of the ATM

Step 2: Select the language and choose GENERATE ATM PIN option

Step 3: Click on GENERATE OTP to receive OTP & request ID on your registered Mobile number

Step 4: Insert your debit card once more and again select GENERATE ATM PIN option

Step 5: Now select VALIDATE OTP and enter the Request ID & OTP received on your registered Mobile Number

Step 6: Enter 4 digits of your choice & re-enter the same to set it as your new ATM PIN

---

**Option 3) Through Internet Banking**

Step 1: Log into your internet banking account and go to CARDS tab.

Step 2: Click on GENERATE INSTANT ATM PIN

Step 3: Select the Debit Card Number, reason for PIN generation and then Click on 'Generate OTP'

Step 4: Enter the OTP received on your registered Mobile Number.

Step 5: Set a PIN of your choice and enter Transaction Password to confirm.

Step 6: Use your Debit cum ATM card with the new PIN set by you at any Bank ATM/POS machine to activate it.

---

**Option 4)through Go mobile+ app**

Step 1: Log into IDBI Bank Go mobile+ app with your MPIN

Step 2: Click on "Debit Card" on the home screen

Step 3: Select "Debit Card PIN generation"

Step 4: From the drop down choose the debit card for which PIN has to be generated, input Remarks and confirm

Step 5: Enter the OTP sent on your regd. Mobile number and create a PIN of your choice.

---

**Option 5) Through Missed Call**

Step 1: Give a missed call to our Toll-free number 18008431144 from the mobile number registered with the Bank

Step 2: You will receive OTP & request ID on your registered Mobile number. OTP will be valid only for 30 minutes.

Step 3: Please go to the nearest IDBI Bank ATM, insert your Debit Card and select "Generate ATM PIN"

Step 4: Select Validate OTP and enter the OTP & Request ID received on your registered Mobile Number

Step 5: Create a new PIN of your choice & confirm

The PIN will be activated instantly.

---

**Option 6) Through SMS**

Step 1: Type GREENPIN Last 6 digits of your Debit Card in the textbox and send it to 9820346920 OR 9821043718 from the mobile number registered with the Bank

Step 2: You will receive OTP & request ID on your registered Mobile number. OTP will be valid only for 30 minutes.

Step 3: Visit the nearest IDBI Bank ATM ,insert your Debit Card and select "Generate ATM PIN"

Step 4: Select Validate OTP and enter the OTP & Request ID received on your registered Mobile Number

The PIN will be activated instantly.

How do I claim refund for failed transactions done through my IDBI Bank account?

Transactions carried out using digital banking channels may fail due to various reasons. Sometimes, the amount is automatically reversed to your account. In case the amount does not get reversed, please follow the process given below for different payment modes.

Debit Cards:

Step 1: If you happen to make a payment through your IDBI Bank debit cum ATM card on any website or swiped the card on a merchant terminal and the transaction is declined or failed, yet the amount is deducted from your account, please note that as per existing process, the amount gets automatically reversed within 10 working days from the date of transaction if the merchant does not claim it.

Step 2: In case after 10 working days also the amount is not reversed to your account, kindly write to us on [customercare@idbi.co.in](mailto:customercare@idbi.co.in) with correct details of the transaction viz. date, merchant name, amount, payment reference id, copy of void slip, proof of payment made via other modes, etc.

Please note: You may also visit your nearest IDBI Bank branch and submit a written application to initiate chargeback for the failed transaction.

Internet or Mobile Banking

Step 1: In case of failed or unsuccessful transactions carried out through IDBI Bank internet or mobile banking facility viz. bill payments, fund transfer, mobile or DTH recharge, etc., the amount automatically gets reversed to your account in 72 working hours or 3 working days.

Step 2: if the amount does not reflect in your account after expiry of this timeline, please register a case by calling our phone banking on 1800-209-4324or 1800-22-1070 (toll free) if overseas call 0091-2267719100(call charges apply). You can also write to us at [customercare@idbi.co.in](https://www.idbi.bank.in/customercare@idbi.co)

IMPS/UPI  

transactions like IMPS (Immediate Payment Service) carried out through internet & mobile banking, USSD or UPI apps are facilitated by National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI).

In case any of your IMPS/UPI payments have failed, please do the following:

Step 1:For IMPS payment through internet banking, check the status of the transaction and the remarks given there.

step 2: As per existing process, settlement of IMPS/UPI transactions happens in 2 working days. Request you to wait for this period to expire.

step 3: if you are the sender of funds, please check with the beneficiary for the receipt of amount in his bank account.

Step 4: if the amount has not automatically reversed to your account, the beneficiary has to take up the matter with his/her bank with the Retrieval Reference Number (RRN).

Step 5: If you are the receiver of funds from other bank and amount does not get credited to your IDBI Bank account or is not reversed to remitter bank even after 2 working days, please lodge a case with our phone banking on 1800-209-4324or 1800-22-1070 or write to us at [customercare@idbi.co.in](https://www.idbi.bank.in/customercare@idbi.co)

How do I link Aadhaar to my IDBI Bank account?

**IDBI Bank** provides you with multiple channels to verify your Aadhaar number to your existing account(s). You may choose from any one of the following options:

**Option 1: through our website**

Step 1: Visit [www.idbibank.in](https://www.idbi.bank.in/) and click on "Aadhaar verification"

Step 2: Enter your Customer ID and 12-digit Aadhaar number

Step 3: Enter the OTP sent by UIDAI on the mobile number updated in your Aadhaar

Step 4: Submit the information

Step 5: in 3-5 working days, you shall receive an sms informing you of acceptance or rejection of your Aadhaar verification request.

**Option 2: through self service on phone banking IVR:**

Step 1: Dial toll free numbers 1800-209-4324or 1800-22-1070 & select your preferred language

Step 2: in the main menu, go to "Banking Accounts"

Step 3: Choose your action "Aadhaar Verification" or Aadhaar Seeding"

Step 4: Follow the IVR voice and enter the Customer ID and Aadhaar number when prompted

Step 4: Enter the OTP received on the mobile number registered in your Aadhaar

Step 5: if all the details match as mandated by UIDAI, your account will be authenticated with Aadhaar instantly

If you are unable to verify your Aadhaar with any of the above options, please visit the nearest IDBI Bank branch to carry out biometric e-KYC.

Please note that Aadhaar verification and Aadhaar seeding are different. While Aadhaar verification is carried out for all accounts you hold in the bank, Aadhaar seeding can be done only with one single savings account pan India.

Aadhaar seeding is beneficial for receiving Direct Benefit Transfers like govt. subsidies and for using Aadhaar Payment Bridge System (APBS).

If you wish to seed or link any of your IDBI Bank savings accounts with your Aadhaar number, the following options are available:

* I-net Banking: Log into your internet banking account, click on "REQUESTS" tab and then on "Aadhaar linking request"
* Phone Banking: Dial our phone banking numbers 18002094324or 1800221070, go to Banking Accounts, select Aadhar Linking and then opt for Aadhaar seeding .
* Go mobile+ app: You can also submit a request to seed your bank account with Aadhaar number through the "Requests" tab in IDBI Bank Go mobile+ app for Android & IOS devices

ATM: You may visit any IDBI Bank ATM, insert your debit card, enter your PIN and select "OTHERS"

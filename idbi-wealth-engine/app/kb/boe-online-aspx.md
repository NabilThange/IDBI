# Overview

‘BOE-*Online*’ service, is self-service digital window on GoTrade+ portal on Corporate Net Banking to handle with faster turnaround import related payments and Bill of Entry regularization in IDPMS. You can give import payment instructions and provide BOE regularization all in a single request instance and all on the same page.

‘BOE-*Online*’ includes:

* **BOE-Pay:** You can select outstanding BOE(s) and initiate import payment for seamless reporting of BOE settlement.
* **BOE-Regularize:** You can simply match outstanding BOE(s) with import payments (ORM) in a transparent manner with reduced error and paperless process.

# Key benefits of ‘BOE-*Online*’ Services

* Paperless online transaction for lodgment of Foreign direct Import Bills;
* Dynamic Dashboard for customers giving comprehensive view of outstanding Bill of Entries (BOE) and import payments in IDPMS.
* BOE regularization by matching and knocking off outstanding advance remittances with the outstanding BOE
* Faster turnaround in regularization of BOE and regulatory compliance.
* Online generation of BOE acknowledgements

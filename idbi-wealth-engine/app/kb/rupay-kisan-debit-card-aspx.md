# RuPayKisan Card

# About The Card

Cash withdrawal limit of Rs.25,000/- and purchase at Point of Sale (POS) limit of Rs. 50,000/- in a day.

# Features

* Ministry of Agriculture's initiative towards the credit requirements of agriculture sector.
* Kisan Credit Card Scheme aims at providing need based and timely credit support to the farmers for their
  cultivation needs as well as non-farm activities in a cost effective manner.

* Farmers under this scheme are issued a IDBI RuPay Kisan Card on their account.
* The cards can currently be used at both ATMs and POS machines.

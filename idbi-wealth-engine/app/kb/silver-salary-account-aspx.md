# IDBI Bank Silver Salary Account Facilities

Our Silver Salary Account has been specially designed keeping your requirements in mind. With this account you can expect banking anywhere, anytime. You can avail of special privileges, easier and quicker transactions, and value-added services to enjoy a renewed experience of convenient and hassle-free banking. As an esteemed Silver Salary Account holder you will be entitled to loads of benefits.

# IDBI Bank Silver Account Features

* **Rupay Platinum Debit Card-** Get a free Rupay Platinum Debit card and set up direct debit for all your expenses
* **Insurance Cover on Debit Card -** Personal Accident covers upto Rs.5 Lakhs and Purchase Protection upto Rs.50,000 upto 90 days
* **Demand Drafts and Pay Orders-**  We provides concession in Demand drafts and pay orders

* Upto Rs.15 Lakhs of Air Accident / Personal Accident / Disability Insurance Cover on account level
* **Hassel free banking-** We have specialized representatives for our preferred customers to execute your banking needs more easily.
* **Electronic Fund Transfers facility-** RTGS, NEFT, IMPS Fund Transfer services are available with it

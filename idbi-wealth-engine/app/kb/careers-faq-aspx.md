##### FAQs

I am very interested to work in an esteemed organization like IDBI Bank. Please give me the opportunity to prove myself.

The link [Current Openings](idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx) will take you to all the vacancies/ positions that are being filled by the Bank. You can click on the [Current Openings](idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx) to check out the detailed description of the position.

How to apply for a job in IDBI Bank?

The process for application for jobs in IDBI Bank is channelized through the link [Current Openings](idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx), which lists all the vacancies/ positions that are being filled by the Bank. You can click on the link [Current Openings](idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx) to check out the detailed description of the position.

What is the selection process for entry into IDBI Bank?

The link [Current Openings](idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx) provides the details of selection process for each requirement that the Bank has. The detailed selection process can be found in the respective vacancy/ position of the concerned Recruitment Process.

Who is eligible to apply to IDBI Bank?

Being a universal bank, we offer a diverse range of financial products and services. We need bright young graduates, post graduates, and professionally qualified CAs/ MBAs to carry the business objectives forward. The eligibility criteria(s) are enumerated in the respective vacancy/ position of the selection process under [Current Openings](idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx).

Where can I submit my resume or find out about current openings, etc. in this organization?

Selection is through open recruitment and the link [Current Openings](idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx) would provide you the details of current openings in the Bank.

I am a graduate. I want to pursue a career in Banking as I feel that there is a good scope for career growth and opportunities in this sector. Please advise me if I can find a suitable opportunity in IDBI Bank.

You can join the Bank in the cadre of Executives/ Junior Assistant Manager at entry level. Keep track of advertisements in the prominent newspapers to find out about the openings in IDBI Bank. For openings, click [Current Openings](idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx) to get the details.

What kind of a career path can I expect in IDBI Bank?

IDBI Bank has distinct business segments like Retail Banking, Corporate Banking, Agriculture Finance, Medium, SME Business, Treasury, etc. Further, there are also other varied fields like Risk, Finance & Accounts, IT & MIS, Security, Infrastructure Management Department (IMD), etc. Cross-functional movements are strongly encouraged to build capability in each employee to become a knowledgeable and versatile individual to provide the necessary service and enhance his/her professional skills.

What is the work culture at IDBI Bank?

IDBI Bank has a very strong balance sheet and it is one of the fastest growing Bank. All its branches run on state-of-the art Core banking system. Work environment is very professional but also very friendly. To groom the right candidates’ early responsibility is given which enables independent decision-making, thus helping the individual to reach his/her potential. People with good performance are acknowledged and rewarded suitably.

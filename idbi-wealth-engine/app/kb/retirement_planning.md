# Retirement Planning - IDBI Bank

## Retirement Solutions

### 1. National Pension System (NPS)
- Regulated by PFRDA
- Tax benefits under 80CCD(1B): ₹50,000
- Low cost: 0.01% fund management charges
- Choice of fund managers
- Market-linked returns (10-12% historical)

### 2. Senior Citizens Savings Scheme (SCSS)
- Eligibility: 60 years+
- Interest rate: 8.2% p.a.
- Maximum investment: ₹30 lakh (per person)
- Tenure: 5 years (extendable by 3 years)
- Quarterly interest payout
- Tax deduction under 80C

### 3. Annuity Plans
- Monthly pension for life
- Options: Life annuity, joint life, guaranteed period
- Rates: 6-7% p.a. based on age

### 4. Monthly Income Schemes
- Fixed monthly income
- Tenure: 3-10 years
- Returns: 7-8% p.a.

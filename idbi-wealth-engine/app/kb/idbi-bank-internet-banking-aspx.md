# How To Apply

![Eligibility Salaried](images/Retail-Omni-iNet-Banking.jpg)

##### Retail Internet Banking / Internet Banking Lite (Omni Channel)

New to iNet Banking?

Register online in minutes:

* Click “First Time User / Register Now” on the login page
* Enter your account details and choose access type
* Verify with OTP and debit card credentials
* Set your login and transaction passwords

Don’t have a debit card?

Visit your nearest IDBI Bank branch and submit the Channel Registration Form to activate Net Banking.

![Eligibility Salaried](images/Corporate-Internet-Banking.jpg)

##### Corporate Internet Banking

To activate Corporate iNet Banking, submit a duly filled Corporate Registration Form (CRF) at your IDBI Bank home branch. The form must be signed by authorized signatories as per your entity type (e.g., directors, partners, trustees, etc.).

Access is granted based on entity type and authorization—view-only, maker, or full transaction rights.

##### Soft Token

IDBI Bank Soft Token is a secure, app-based alternative to SMS OTPs for authenticating your transactions/requests. It allows you to instantly generate OTPs on your mobile device, even without network connectivity, ensuring uninterrupted access and enhanced security.

To explore the complete registration and activation process, kindly refer the **[Soft Token Manual](pdf/Soft-token-Registration-Process-Guide.pdf)**.

If you have any queries related to IDBI Bank Soft Token, you can refer **[Soft Token FAQs](pdf/Soft-Token-Based-OTP-FAQ.pdf)**.

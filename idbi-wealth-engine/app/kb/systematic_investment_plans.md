# IDBI Systematic Investment Plans (SIP)

## What is SIP?
A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It's a disciplined approach to wealth creation through rupee cost averaging.

## Benefits of SIP
1. **Rupee Cost Averaging**: Buy more units when price is low, fewer when high
2. **Power of Compounding**: Long-term wealth multiplication
3. **Disciplined Investing**: Regular automated investments
4. **Flexibility**: Start, stop, or modify SIP anytime
5. **Affordable**: Start with as low as ₹500/month

## How to Start SIP with IDBI
1. Complete KYC (Know Your Customer) process
2. Choose mutual fund scheme based on risk profile
3. Select SIP amount and date
4. Provide bank mandate for auto-debit
5. Start investing

## Tax Benefits
- ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme) SIP eligible for Section 80C deduction
- Long-term capital gains (after 1 year): 10% tax on gains above ₹1 lakh
- Short-term capital gains (within 1 year): 15% tax

## Top-up SIP

- Recommended increase: 10-15% per year
- Accelerates wealth creation significantly

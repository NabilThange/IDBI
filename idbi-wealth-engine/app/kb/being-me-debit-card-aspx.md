# IDBI Bank VISA Being Me PayWave Debit Card

# About The Card

* Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 25000/- and purchase at Point of Sale (POS) limit of Rs. 25000/- in a day.
* 1 loyalty point for every Rs. 100 spent on the Debit Card
* Simply wave/tap the card on the contactless Point of Sale (PoS) and pay without entering PIN for purchases upto Rs. 5000.

##### Features

Daily Limits

Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 40000/- and purchase at Point of Sale (PoS) limit of Rs. 40000/- in a day.

International Validity

The Classic Debit Card can be used both in India and abroad to make purchases at Merchant locations and withdraw local currency at ATMs.
The customer is debited in INR. All transactions are reflected in the account statement in INR and merchant location details are
captured

Discounts at merchant establishments

We make tie-ups with different merchants from time to time to offer discounts to our Debit Card holders. Customers can avail of
this discount by making purchases through the Debit Card at these merchant locations.

Online transactions

The Debit Card can be used online, for shopping, booking air/rail/movie tickets & utility bill payments.

Insurance Cover\*

Customers are covered with an insurance cover upto Rs. 1 lac for lost and counterfeit cards.

*\*Effective 1st July 2013, for Insurance claims to be accepted and processed; there should be a minimum of 2 purchase
transactions using the Debit Card in last 3 months prior to the event date.*

Loyalty Program#

Customers will gain one loyalty points for every Rs.100 spent on the Debit Card.

*#Loyalty point reward structure as well as reward of loyalty points for different merchant categories is
subject to revision from time to time.*

##### Features and Benefits

Earn While You Spend

The Co-branded Lumine Platinum Credit Card understands your lifestyle and rewards you for your needs. You will earn 3 Delight Points for every Rs. 100 spent by you using your Eclat Credit Card. So enjoy extra rewards on travelling, shopping, dine-out or watching movies! Get rewarded for all your spends! Additionally, enjoy bonus delight points as following:

* 500 bonus delight points on card activation.
* 500 bonus delight points on first transaction of Rs. 2,000 or more within 30 days of card activation.
* Additional milestone benefit of 1,000 points on net annual spends of Rs. 1 lakhs.
* Additional milestone benefit of 1,500 points on net annual spends of Rs. 2 lakhs.

**\*Delight points would be accrued only on transactions except the ones exempted from delight points accrual. Refer the exempted transaction categories [here.](https://www.idbidelight.com/content/faq)**

Interest Free Credit

Enjoy Interest free credit of up to 48 days\* on your purchases and manage your payments as per your convenience. You can choose to pay the total outstanding amount, minimum amount due or any other amount higher than the minimum amount due.
\*Refer MITC for more details

Drive On

Waiver of 1%\* fuel surcharge every time you fill fuel using Eclat Select Card across all fuel stations for transactions in the range of Rs. 400 to Rs. 4000. So keep driving and enjoying with friends and family.
  
\*Maximum waiver of upto Rs. 300 per month

Insurance Covers

Co-branded Lumine Platinum Credit Card provides Personal Accidental Death Insurance cover of Rs.2 lakhs & Credit Shield Cover.

Wider Acceptance

Get more freedom with your Eclat Select Credit Card. You can use it at over 5.81 million merchants in India and over 44 million merchants abroad.

Zero Lost Card Liability

In case of loss of credit card, report it immediately at our 24-Hour Credit Card Customer Care No: 1800-209-4324 (Toll Free) / 022-67719100 (Non-toll free). The card will be immediately blocked for all transactions to prevent card misuse. Any fraudulent transaction after reporting the loss will be covered by the Bank.

Family Cards

You may apply for upto 1 add-on card for your family members.

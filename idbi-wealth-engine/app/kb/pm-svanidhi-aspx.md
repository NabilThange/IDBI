# PM Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi)

Government of India (GoI) on 14.05.2020 announced PM Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi) Scheme for
providing affordable loans to street vendors. The Scheme is available to all street vendors engaged in vending in Urban
areas as on or before March 24, 2020

The details are as mentioned in the table below:

| Sr. No | Field | Content |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Eligible Segments | The Scheme is available to all street vendors engaged in vending in Urban areas as on or before March 24, 2020. |
| 2 | Facility | Term Loan |
| 3 | Purpose | To facilitate working capital loan up to Rs.10,000 |
| 4 | Loan Amount | Maximum Rs.10000/- per borrower |
| 5 | Tenor / Validity | To be repaid in 12 Equated Monthly Installments. |
| 6 | Pricing | Linked  to banks Repo Linked Lending Rate (RLLR) and Rating |
| 7 | Security | NIL |
| 8 | Margin | NIL |
| 9 | Guarantee | To be covered under CGTMSE on portfolio basis. |

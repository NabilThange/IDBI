# IDBI Bank Fixed Deposits

## Overview
IDBI Bank offers Fixed Deposits with attractive interest rates and flexible tenures. FDs are a safe investment option with guaranteed returns.

## Interest Rates (As of July 2026)

### Regular Customers
- 7 days to 45 days: 3.50% p.a.
- 46 days to 90 days: 4.00% p.a.
- 91 days to 180 days: 5.25% p.a.
- 181 days to less than 1 year: 6.00% p.a.
- 1 year to less than 2 years: 7.00% p.a.
- 2 years to less than 3 years: 7.25% p.a.
- 3 years to less than 5 years: 7.50% p.a.
- 5 years and above: 7.75% p.a.

### Senior Citizens (Additional 0.50%)
Senior citizens (60 years and above) get an additional 0.50% interest on all tenure options.
- 1 year to less than 2 years: 7.50% p.a.
- 2 years to less than 3 years: 7.75% p.a.
- 3 years to less than 5 years: 8.00% p.a.
- 5 years and above: 8.25% p.a.

## Minimum and Maximum Amount
- Minimum deposit: ₹10,000
- Maximum deposit: No limit

## Tenure Options
- Minimum: 7 days
- Maximum: 10 years
- Flexible tenure options available

## Tax Saving Fixed Deposit
Special 5-year tax saving FD under Section 80C:
- Interest rate: 7.75% p.a.
- Lock-in period: 5 years
- Tax deduction: Up to ₹1.5 lakh under Section 80C
- Premature withdrawal: Not allowed

## Features
1. **Auto-renewal**: Option to automatically renew FD on maturity
2. **Loan facility**: Get loan up to 90% of deposit amount
3. **Nomination facility**: Add nominees for safety
4. **Online booking**: Open FD through internet banking or mobile app
5. **Quarterly interest payout**: Option for regular income

## Premature Withdrawal
- Allowed after 7 days from date of deposit
- Penalty: 1% on applicable interest rate
- No penalty for premature withdrawal in case of death of depositor

## Documents Required
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Address proof
- Passport size photograph
- KYC compliance

## How to Open
1. Visit nearest IDBI Bank branch
2. Fill FD application form
3. Submit KYC documents
4. Deposit amount via cash/cheque/online transfer
5. Get FD receipt

## TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)
- TDS applicable if interest exceeds ₹40,000 per year (₹50,000 for senior citizens)
- Form 15G/15H can be submitted if total income is below taxable limit

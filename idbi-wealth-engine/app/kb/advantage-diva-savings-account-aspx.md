##### Woman Savings Account | Advantage DIVA Savings Account

IDBI Bank is introducing a special savings account for women, which we have coined as Advantage DIVA Savings Account. Along with this, we are also offering a zero balance savings account absolutely free for her child below the age of eighteen years.

* MAB – All locations Rs. 10,000/-
* One zero balance account for your child below the age of 18 years

* Higher ATM cash withdrawal Limits- Rs. 40,000/- per day
* Personalized PAP Cheque Book

* Upto 50% Discount \* on locker Services

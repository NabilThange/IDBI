##### The Right To Information Act, 2005

###### **The names, designations and other particulars of the public Information officers**

All the Branch Heads of the Bank as also an officer in the Bank’s staff college have been designated as Central Assistant Public Information Officers (CAPIOs). All functional Heads of the Bank have been appointed and designated as Central Public Information Officers (CPIOs) of the Bank.

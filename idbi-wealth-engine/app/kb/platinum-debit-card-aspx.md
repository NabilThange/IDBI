# IDBI Bank VISA Platinum PayWave Debit Card

  

##### for those who deserve better than the best.

# About The Card

* Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 1 lakh and purchase limit at Point of Sale (POS) of Rs. 2 lakh in a day.
* 2 loyalty points for every Rs. 100 spent at merchant establishments
* 2 Free visits per calendar quarter at participating Airport lounges in India.

##### Features

Free Airport Lounge Access

IDBI BANK Platinum card offers 2 free visits per calendar quarter at participating Airport Lounges in India. For List of participating lounges please visit <https://www.visa.co.in/content/dam/VCOM/regional/ap/india/global-elements/documents/in-participating-airport-lounges.pdf>

Enhanced Daily Limits

Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 1 lakh and purchase limit at Point of Sale (POS) of Rs. 2 lakh in a day.

Loyalty Redemptions#

The customer stands to earn 2 loyalty points for every Rs. 100 spent using the Platinum Debit Card for purchases at merchant establishments.

*#Loyalty point reward structure as well as reward of loyalty points for different merchant categories is subject to revision from time to time.*

Enhanced Insurance Cover\*

In addition to insurance cover for lost cards, you also get personal accident cover,
cover on loss of checked-in baggage, purchase protection, fire and burglary for household content.

* PA cover - Rs. 5 lakh
* Loss of checked baggage - Rs. 50,000
* Purchase Protection - Rs. 20,000
* Fire and Burglary for House Hold Contents - Rs. 50,000

*\*Effective 1st July 2013, for Insurance claims to be accepted and processed; there should be
a minimum of 2 purchase transactions using the Debit Card in last 3 months prior to the event date.*

Discounts at Merchant Establishments

IDBI Bank regularly comes up with attractive offers and discounts at various merchant establishments through which Platinum Debit
Card customers can avail fabulous offers on the Card usage.

##### Overview

A power packed account acting as a stepping stone to the Preferred Banking services and availability of better services .
The facilities and benefits provided are drawn absolutely on your day to day requirement from the Bank.

##### Benefits you can avail by opening a Power Plus Account

You may opt to either maintain an average monthly balance in your savings account of Rs. 50,000 in month or earn higher returns on the
Term Deposit and avail benefits under Power Plus by linking your TD with the savings account. Minimum TD of Rs. 5 lakhs.

Free NEFT through our Netbanking channel.

Any branch cheque deposit and account to account transfer free.

Enhanced limit on cash withdrawal and POS on your International Debit cum ATM card.

25% discount on Locker of any size with the Bank.

First year AMC free if you open a Demat account with the Bank.

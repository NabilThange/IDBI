# Harvesting and transport Loan

[Apply Now](apply-now.aspx)

# Overview

The bank extends financial assistance to individuals, Custom service units (CSU) managed by individuals/institutions / organizations, who maintain Tractors, Trucks, combined threshers, etc., and have entered into an agreement and recommended by sugar units for undertaking the work of Harvesting & Transport of sugarcane.

# Who is eligible for the Loan?

Individuals, Farmers, and Custom service units (CSU) managed by individuals/institutions / organizations, who maintain fleet of Bullock carts, Tractors, Trucks, combined threshers, etc. and have entered into an agreement and recommended by sugar units for undertaking the work of Harvesting & Transport of sugarcane.

# Loan Amount

Term Loan - The loan /advance amount per borrower is worked out on the basis of total bill amount estimated by the borrower.

# Tenor

Term Loan – 9-12 months.

# About The Card

* Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 75,000/- and make purchases worth Rs. 75,000/- in a day at Point of Sale (POS).
* 1 loyalty point for every Rs. 100 spent on the Debit Card

##### Features

High Withdrawal & transaction limit

Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 75,000/- and make purchases worth 75,000/- in a day at Point of Sale (POS).

International Validity

The Gold Debit Card can be used both in India and abroad to make purchases at Merchant locations and withdraw local currency at ATMs. The customer is debited in INR. All transactions are
reflected in the account statement in INR and merchant location details are captured.

Loyalty points

Customers will gain one loyalty point for every Rs. 100 spent on the Debit Card

*#Loyalty point reward structure as well as reward of loyalty points for different merchant categories is subject to revision from time to time.*

Online transactions

Gold Debit Card can be used online, for shopping, booking air/rail/movie tickets & utility bill payments.

Insurance Cover\*

In addition to insurance cover for lost and counterfeit cards upto Rs. 2,00,000/- customers will also be covered for:

* PA cover - Rs. 5 lakh
* Loss of checked baggage - Rs. 50,000
* Purchase Protection - Rs. 20,000
* Fire and Burglary for House Hold Contents - Rs. 50,000

*Effective 1st July 2013, for Insurance claims to be accepted and processed; there should be a minimum of 2 purchase transactions using the Debit Card in last 3 months prior to the event date.*

Discounts at merchant establishments

We make tie-ups with different merchants from time to time to offer discounts to our Gold Debit Card holders. Customers can
avail of this discount by making purchases through the Gold Debit Card at these merchant locations.

Better tracking

Receive instant SMS alerts on transaction made on your Gold Debit Card

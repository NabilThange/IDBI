Customer Risks

While using the Mobile Banking facility, customers should be aware of potential risks such
as malware or phishing attacks, use of unsecured networks, device theft, fake apps, SIM
swap fraud, and unauthorized access due to weak authentication. Customers are advised to
keep their devices secure, use official banking apps, enable strong authentication, avoid
public Wi-Fi, and never share confidential information like passwords or OTPs.

# Why choose LIC CSL IDBI Bank Co-branded Credit Card?

LIC CSL IDBI Bank Credit Cards opens the doors to an unmatched experience powered by exciting offers and innovative financial solutions which suits your lifestyle. The cards offer best-in-class privileges specially crafted to meet your stature. The Credit Cards issued are EMV Chip and PIN enabled for enhanced security so that you can use it freely without any concerns.

Your card has unique built-in Contactless technology that enables you to make fast, convenient and secure payment at reatils outlets. Simply tap your card whereever contacless payments are accepted. You can use your card to make regular swipe transactions where contactless facility is not enabled.

And there’s more, you earn delight points on all your spends using LIC CSL IDBI Bank Credit Card. These points can be redeemed against merchandise, flight booking, recharge, E-vouchers etc..

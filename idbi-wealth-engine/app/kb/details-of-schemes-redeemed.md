Despite IDBI Bank's best efforts, viz. sending individual notices/ reminders to all the bondholders, issuing repeated
advertisements in the newspapers and providing information on the Bank's website, some of the bondholders have not yet
submitted the discharged bond certificates. In terms of the OD, no interest accrues on the redeemed bonds after the call
option/ redemption dates. Bondholders are again requested to forward the duly discharged bond certificates of the
aforesaid schemes to the respective Registrars by quoting their Folio no, present address, contact no. and bank
details of the first holder together with a copy of his/her PAN. Investors eligible for TDS exemption may furnish Form
15G/15H with a copy of PAN which is mandatory as per revised provisions of Income Tax Act.

# Types of Personal Loan

Personal Loan to Salaried Individuals/Pensioners

## Features And Advantages

Eligibility

All salaried customer having corporate salary account account with IDBI BankI. All Confirmed/permanent employees of State/Central/ PSU/ Departments, Multi National Companies, Listed Companies, Reputed Pvt Ltd Companies with or without existing relationship with IDBI Bank. All pensioners having pension account with IDBI Bank.

Minimum salary/Pension

* Rs. 3,60,000/- Per Annum for Salaried Class/Pensioner

Age

* **For salaried:**
  + Minimum Age : 21 Years
  + Maximum age at termination of loan should not be above 60 years or date of retirement, whichever is earlier.
* **For pensioners:**
  + Minimum Age: At the start of pension
  + Maximum Age: 75 years at the termination of loan

Loan Tenure

* Minimum : 12 months
* Maximum : 72 months

Loan Amount

* Minimum Amount : Rs. 50,000/-
* Maximum Amount : Rs. 5,00,000/- (Pensioner) / Rs. 15,00,000 \* (Salaried)   
  \*T&C apply

Interest Type

* Fixed Rate

Processing Fee

* 1 % of the loan amount subject to minimum Rs.2500/- plus applicable taxes.

Part Payment/Foreclosure Charges

* **Foreclosure:**
  + Within Twelve months from the disbursement date:3% of the outstanding loan amount
  + Between 12 months to 24 months from disbursement date:1% of the outstanding loan amount
  + After 24 months : Nil
* **Part payment:**
  + Within Six months from the disbursement :3% of the outstanding loan amount
  + After Six months of disbursement: Nil
  + Maximum part payment allowed is 10 % of the outstanding loan amount subject to minimum of Rs. 10,000/-
  + Part payment allowed maximum 3 times in a year with minimum interval of 90 days.

Top Up Loan

* Top up facility available.

##### Foreign Outward remittance facility (Fx-Remit) on Retail I-Net Banking

Fx-Remit services on I-Net Banking platform offers a highly convenient mode of transfer of funds abroad in a few click creating a wonderful digital experience for customer. Using Fx-Remit under Retail I-Net Banking, customers can initiate hassle free & paperless Foreign Outward Remittance towards education, family maintenance, etc. and also for non-trade transactions viz. consultancy fee, agency commission freight payments, etc. up to USD 25,000/- (or its equivalent). The remittance facility is extended to our NRI customers too.

# Key Features

* **Speed** – No more queues or waiting time
* **Ease** – Paperless and hassle free process
* **Convenience** – Transfers can be made from the comfort of home or office or from any place where Internet access is available

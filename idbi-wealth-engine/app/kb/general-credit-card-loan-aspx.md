# Overview

IDBI GCC Loan is for all individuals engaged in Non-farm entrepreneurial activity.

![](assets/images/royal-benfits.png)
![](assets/images/royal-benfits.png)

##### The product details are as mentioned below:

###### Eligible Segments

All individuals engaged in Non-farm entrepreneurial activity.

###### Facility

Term Loan                  
Cash Credit

###### Purpose

To meet the credit requirement of borrowers.

###### Loan Amount

Minimum: Rs. 50,000/-  
Maximum: Rs. 5,00,000/-

###### Tenor / Validity

Term Loan: Maximum 5 years  
Cash Credit: 12 month, Repayment on demand

###### Pricing

Linked to Bank Base Rate.

###### Security

Cash Credit: Stock & book-debts & all assets created out of Bank loan.  
Term Loan: Assets acquired out of Bank loan.

###### margin

Cash Credit: 25%  
Term Loan: 25%

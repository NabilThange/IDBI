# Overview

Blue Revolution has created good potential for fisheries development in the country.
Bank provides finance to fisheries related activities such as Inland fisheries
(construction of fish ponds, purchase of seeds, feeds etc.), Marine fisheries
(in-shore, off-shore, deep sea fishing) and brackish water scheme. Individual,
fishermans, co-op societies, companies can avail loan.

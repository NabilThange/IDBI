# Instant bank account updates through SMS & email

[Apply Now](apply-now.aspx)

##### Who can apply

To register for Account Alerts, all you need to have is a Savings or Current Account (for sole proprietorship, partnership and other corporates) with us.

**How to apply for IDBI AccountAlerts?**  
Just ask for the AccountAlerts application form available at all our branches and ATMs or download it. Tick on the alerts you would like to receive and we’ll take care of the rest.
  
  
Or, simply call our [Phone Banking numbers](idbi-bank-phone-banking-no.aspx) and the application form will be delivered right at your doorstep. You can click in 'login' and register online using Internet Banking.

##### Host to Host Connectivity

We offer Host to Host Connectivity with our clients’ ERP Systems for seamless transfer of instruction to our host systems and online status update in the clients’ systems.

##### Brief Features:

* Date Extraction from ERP and Auto / Manual file Upload in Bank’s Portal

* Auto Upload of file generated by ERP in Bank’s Portal

* Seamless flow between corporate ERP Database to CMS Database with no manual intervention.

* Improved Security (No interim transmission files written to disk)

* Reduced operational risk

* Better transactional transparency

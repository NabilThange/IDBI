# Notice to Fixed Deposit Customers

**Notice to Fixed Deposit Customers**

As per guidelines of Reserve Bank of India (RBI), if term deposit accounts are opened with operating instructions as Either or Survivor, Any One or Survivor and Former or Survivor, a specific mandate is required to be obtained for such accounts, in order to facilitate the term deposit account holders for prepayment of deposit to **anyone of the account holders** at any time during the term of the deposit.

For hassle free prepayment of term deposit to anyone of the account holders, it is advised that you may please submit the specific mandate form duly filled and signed by all the deposit holders ( please click the link provided at the end of this notice to download the “Specific Mandate” form).

In case of any further queries, you may call or visit any nearby Branch.

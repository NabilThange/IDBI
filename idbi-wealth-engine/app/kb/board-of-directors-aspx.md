### Mr. Rakesh Sharma

Managing Director & CEO

* ![](assets/images/location.svg)
  Mumbai

Mr. Rakesh Sharma is a seasoned banker with a total stint of over 40 years in various Banks. He started his banking career in State Bank of India (SBI) and held various responsibilities in SBI. He then moved to Lakshmi Vilas Bank Ltd. as MD & CEO and served there for a period of 18 months. He retired from Canara Bank as MD & CEO after serving for a period of 3 years, in July 2018. He has expertise in Accountancy, Agriculture & Rural Economy, Banking, Economics, Small Scale Industry, HR, Business Management, Administration and Corporate Governance.

### Mr. Jayakumar Subramonia Pillai

Deputy Managing Director

* ![](assets/images/location.svg)

### Mr. Sumit Phakka

Deputy Managing Director

* ![](assets/images/location.svg)
  Mumbai

Shri Sumit Phakka is a senior banking professional with almost 30 years of experience in State Bank of India (SBI) in various capacities and geographies, viz., New Delhi, Chandigarh, Mumbai, Toronto with substantial exposure to branch banking and credit delivery to small and medium enterprises. Before joining IDBI Bank, he was working as Chief General Manager in SBI, New Delhi. He has also worked in various scales in Corporate Accounts Group in Mumbai and is experienced in branch banking, mobilization of retail resources as well as credit delivery in sectors like Retail, Agriculture, MSME, Recovery, etc. He was the Vice President (credit) at SBI’s Canadian Operations for five years where he managed the corporate credit portfolio of the Bank in Canada, which included marketing for new deals including bilateral deals and syndicate advances as well as India based deals and maintaining credit quality. He has expertise in Accountancy, Agriculture and Rural Economy, Banking, Finance, Small-Scale Industry, Business Management, Administration and Corporate Governance.

### Mr. Sat Pal Bhanoo

LIC Nominee Director

* ![](assets/images/location.svg)
  Mumbai

Mr. Sat Pal Bhanoo took charge as the Managing Director of Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) in July 2023 and ceased to be MD of LIC on December 31, 2025. He joined the Corporation as Special Batch Direct Recruit Officer in October, 1988. He holds a Bachelor's Degree in History (Honors) from Himachal Pradesh University, Shimla. He has a varied and rich marketing experience. He served the Corporation in various roles as Zonal Manager (Central Zone), Additional Director, Zonal Training Centre (Bhopal), Regional Manager (P&IR), North Zone (Delhi). As Chief (Marketing-SBA), he looked after the Marketing portfolio of Senior Business Associates & Life Insurance Corporation Associates as well, with a focus on revolutionizing marketing activities and empowering them. He has expertise in Human Resource, Business Management, Administration and Corporate Governance.

### Mr. Ajay Prakash Sawhney

Independent Director

* ![](assets/images/location.svg)
  New Delhi

Shri Ajay Prakash Sawhney, a Graduate of Institute of Mechanical Engineers, London, UK (equivalent of B.E. Mechanical Engineer), is a 1984 batch Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer of Andhra Pradesh cadre. He has served as Secretary to the Government of India, at the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology and retired on February 28, 2022. As Secretary, Ministry of Electronics and IT, Government of India from June 2017 to February 2022, Shri Sawhney led the Digidhan Mission for promotion of UPI and digital payments, guided the growth of Aadhaar ecosystem and multiple national public digital platforms like Digilocker, Umang, Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (Health), Bhashini (language technologies) and helped significant growth and acceleration of electronic manufacturing in India. He actively catalysed Indian technology start-ups and Atmanirbhar Bharat Apps, supervised cyber law and cyber security, Data Protection Bill, design of Indian micro-processors, supercomputing and the launch of the National Semiconductor Mission. He has expertise in Information Technology, Payment & Settlements, Human Resource, Administration and Corporate Governance.

### Shri Abhijit Chakravorty

Additional Director (Independent Category)

* ![](assets/images/location.svg)
  Bhopal

Shri Abhijit Chakravorty is a postgraduate and is a Certified Associate of the Indian Institute of Bankers (CAIIB). He is a seasoned banker and has rich experience of more than 3 decades in SBI. He superannuated as Dy. Managing Director from SBI and his last assignment was as MD&CEO, SBI Cards & Payment Systems Ltd, the publicly listed credit card subsidiary of SBI. Apart from handling assignments in Retail, International Banking and IT verticals of SBI, had exposure of 11 years in high value Corporate Credit involving Infrastructure, Power, Steel, Edible Oil etc, in the Commercial and Mid Corporate Credit vertical of SBI. He also managed the operations of the SBI Global IT Centre and at other senior levels for a period of four years. Shri Chakravorty has led the operations of SBI in Bangladesh as CEO and Country Head and also served at SBI’s Hongkong branch. He has skills/professional knowledge/expertise in the fields of Banking, Finance, Small-Scale Industry, HR, Risk, Information Technology, Payment & Settlement, Business Management, Administration and Corporate governance.

### Shri Ketan Vikamsey

Additional Director (Independent Category)

* ![](assets/images/location.svg)
  Mumbai

Shri Ketan Vikamsey is a Chartered Accountant and Senior Partner at KKC & Associates LLP since 1990. He leads the Assurance Practice, with extensive experience in audits of large corporates, banks, financial institutions, mutual funds, NBFCs, and ARCs, along with regulatory inspections and investigations. He has held key leadership roles, including Chairman of HLB India Federation, and has been associated with ICAI committees and expert groups. A regular speaker at professional forums, he has also contributed to publications on auditing and banking standards. He has served as Director in various companies including as Independent Director and Chairman of the Audit Committee at State Bank of India. He has skills/professional knowledge/expertise in the fields of accountancy, Banking, Finance, Risk, Information Technology, Administration and Corporate Governance.

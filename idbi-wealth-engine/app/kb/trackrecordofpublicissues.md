Track record of the public issues managed by IDBI Bank Ltd.

                  as a Merchant Banker in the last 3 financial years

Securities and Exchange Board of India vide its circular No.CIR/MIRSD/1/2012 dated
January 10, 2012 has mandated certain information with respect to track record of the
public issues managed by a Merchant Banker in the past 3 financial years, to be
disclosed on the website of the Merchant Banker.

“IDBI Bank has not managed any public issue of Equity/Debt as Merchant Banker in the
last 3 financial years”

##### The Right To Information Act, 2005

###### **The powers and duties of its officers and employees**

Powers of Officers and employees are determined and delegated from time to time based on the prevailing organisational structure, current business objectives, regulatory guidelines etc. The powers cover both business and administrative needs.
Duties of officers and employees are likewise determined from time to time based on the role, vertical, place of posting etc.

### Short Term Loans

[Corporate Service Charges](corp-service-charges.aspx)

##### Overview

Short Term Loans (STL) is sanctioned to existing clients with investment grade rating,
having good track record of relationships. STL is generally granted for meeting short-term
cash flow mismatches or as bridge finance against financial closure,
the take out is envisaged from the RTL, to be sanctioned, at times, by the Bank.

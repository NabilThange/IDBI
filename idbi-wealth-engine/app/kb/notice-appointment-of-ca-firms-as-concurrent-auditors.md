Disclaimer Notice

                 Appointment of Concurrent Auditors for IDBI Bank Ltd.

       It has come to IDBI Bank Ltd.'s attention that some unscrupulous persons / agencies have

been approaching Chartered Accountant Firms for confirmed allotments of assignment for

Concurrent Audit by charging commission.

       It may please be noted that the Bank has not hired the services of any agency or

individuals to collect any money/commission/charges for allotment of Concurrent Audit

Assignments. CA Firms are hereby advised to exercise due caution while dealing with such

unscrupulous persons/agencies and any transaction with such agencies, if being done, is at their

own risk. IDBI Bank is not responsible for any loss incurred by the CA Firms.

       It may be noted that the allotments for the Concurrent Audit Assignments for the Bank is

usually finalized in the month of May/ June only for the audit cycle of July to June and whenever

the Bank decides to include any Auditee Unit under Concurrent Audit.

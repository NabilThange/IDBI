# Overview

IDBI Bank GREEN PIN is a paperless PIN generation solution that enables IDBI
Bank Debit Cardholders to securely generate their Debit card PIN in electronic form
through multiple delivery channels. Green PIN as the name suggests, helps save paper thus
contributing to earth’s environment as well as contributes to reduce the carbon foot
print. Green PIN initiative offers a hassle free mechanism to generate Debit-cum-ATM PIN
for existing as well as new Debit card customers. Customers can set their PIN for new
debit cards instantly after receiving the debit card or make use of this functionality in
case they forget their existing Debit-cum-ATM Card PIN. Customers can easily use this
service without visiting the branch/ contact the customer care and wait for the PIN
mailer.

##### How to Generate Debit Card ATM Pin

(Please Note: You cannot set your last PIN as your new PIN. Please choose different 4-digit PIN.)

Generating your Debit-cum-ATM PIN through IDBI Bank ATM

Green PIN generation through IDBI Bank ATM is a two-step
process. In the first step, customer needs to generate an OTP using IDBI Bank ATM
and in the second step customer needs to validate the OTP and set a PIN of his
choice.

* Insert your Debit Card at IDBI Bank ATM
* Post language selection, select Generate ATM PIN option
* Select Generate OTP option to receive OTP & request ID on your
  registered Mobile number
* Reinsert your Debit Card and again select Generate ATM PIN option
* Select Validate OTP and enter the OTP & Request ID received on your
  registered Mobile Number
* Post successful validation customer will be prompted to create a new
  PIN of his choice

New Debit Card PIN will be generated instantly.

Generating your Debit-cum-ATM PIN through IVR

* Dial IDBI Bank Phone Banking Nos. 18002094324 or 1800-22-1070. For overseas customers please dial 022-67719100.
* Choose the Option to Generate ATM PIN in the main menu of IVR and
  enter Customer ID & Debit Card number for which the PIN needs to be
  generated
* Validate the OTP sent to the registered Mobile Number and create a
  new PIN of your choice

New Debit Card PIN will be generated instantly.

To activate the Card, please use it at any Bank’s ATM/POS machine with
the PIN set by you.

Generate your Debit-cum-ATM PIN through Internet
Banking

* Login to IDBI Bank I-Net Banking
* Click “Menu > Cards > Debit Card Services > Pin Generation” Option.
* Select Debit Card No., Pin re-generation Reason and Click SUBMIT.
* Enter New PIN (Last set PIN not allowed), Confirm New PIN and Enter OTP sent to your registered
  Mobile Number in respective field then click SUBMIT.
* “Debit card pin has been successfully reset” message is displayed.

New Debit Card PIN get generated and card get activated instantly.

Generating your Debit-cum-ATM PIN through SMS

Green PIN generation through SMS is a two-step process. In the
first step, customer needs to generate an OTP by sending SMS to
9820346920/9821043718 and in the second step customer needs to validate the OTP
at IDBI Bank ATM and set a PIN of his choice.

* Type GREENPIN
  Last 6 digits of your Debit Card in the textbox and send it to 9820346920. Alternatively, you may send the same SMS to
  9821043718.
* You will receive OTP & request ID on your registered Mobile number.
  OTP will be valid only for 30 minutes.
* Please go to any IDBI Bank ATM and insert your Debit Card and select
  Generate ATM PIN option
* Select Validate OTP and enter the OTP & Request ID received on your
  registered Mobile Number
* Post successful validation customer will be prompted to create a new
  PIN of his choice

New Debit Card PIN will be generated instantly.

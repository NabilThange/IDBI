# Product Features

* Freedom from maintenance of MAB for first 03 months
* Grouping of Accounts to maintain monthly average balance (MAB) at the family level thereby giving flexibility and fungibility in maintenance of MAB
* Attractive Loyalty Program to earn Reward Points on opening of Accounts and digital Transactions
* Unlimited Free Digital transactions and Free Add on Savings Accounts with Nil MAB

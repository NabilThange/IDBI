# RuPay Classic Debit Card

# About The Card

* Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 25,000/- and make purchases worth Rs. 25,000/- in a day at Point of Sale (POS).
* 1 loyalty point for every Rs. 100 spent on the Debit Card

##### Features

Daily Limits

Withdrawal limits are Rs. 25,000/- at the ATM and Rs. 50,000/- at POS. Limits are per day/per
card and are subject to the available balance in the customer’s account

International Validity

The Classic Debit Card can be used both in India and abroad to make purchases at Merchant locations and withdraw local currency at ATMs.
The customer is debited in INR. All transactions are reflected in the account statement in INR and merchant location details are captured.

Discounts at merchant establishments

We make tie-ups with different merchants from time to time to offer discounts to our Debit Card holders. Customers can avail of
this discount by making purchases through the Debit Card at these merchant locations.

Online transactions

The Debit Card can be used online, for shopping, booking air/rail/movie tickets & utility bill payments.

Better tracking

Receive instant SMS alerts on transaction made on your Card.

Insurance Cover\*

Effective, 01st April' 2020, all existing and new classic card issued to IDBI Savings and Current Account customers will not be eligible for Accidental Death or
Permanent Total Disability Insurance coverage of Rs. 1 lakh as per communication received from NPCI vide circular no. “NPCI/2019-20/RuPay/003 dated 23rd May, 2019”.

Loyalty Program#

Customers will gain one loyalty point for every Rs. 100 spent on the Debit Card.

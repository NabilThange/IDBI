# IDBI Bank Salary Account Features

* **Signature Debit Card -** Get a free Signature Debit card and set up direct debit for all your expenses
* **Insurance Cover on Debit Card -** Personal Accident covers upto Rs.10 Lakhs and Purchase Protection upto Rs.2,00,000 upto 90 days
* **Demand Drafts and Pay Orders -**  We provides concession in Demand drafts and pay orders

* Upto Rs.25 Lakhs of Air Accident / Personal Accident / Disability Insurance Cover on account level
* **Hassel free banking -** We have specialized representatives for our preferred customers to execute your banking needs more easily.
* **Electronic Fund Transfers facility -** RTGS, NEFT, IMPS Fund Transfer services are available with it

# Gold Investment Options - IDBI Bank

## Overview
IDBI Bank offers multiple ways to invest in gold - Digital Gold, Gold ETFs, and Sovereign Gold Bonds. Gold is a traditional hedge against inflation and portfolio diversifier.

## 1. Digital Gold

### Features
- Buy gold online starting from ₹100
- Stored in secured vaults
- 99.99% pure gold (24 karat)
- Delivery option available (minimum 1 gram)
- No making charges for physical delivery

### Current Rates (July 2026)
- Buy Price: ₹6,450 per gram
- Sell Price: ₹6,420 per gram
- Spread: ~0.5%

### Benefits
- Start with small amounts
- No storage hassle
- Instant liquidity - sell anytime
- Can convert to physical gold or jewelry

### How to Buy
1. Login to IDBI mobile app or net banking
2. Navigate to Digital Gold section
3. Enter amount or grams
4. Make payment
5. Gold credited to your vault

## 2. Gold ETFs (Exchange Traded Funds)

### IDBI Gold ETF
- Listed on NSE and BSE
- Each unit represents 1 gram of gold
- NAV updates real-time based on gold prices
- Expense ratio: 0.50% per annum
- Minimum investment: 1 unit (~₹6,450)

### Benefits
- Demat form - no physical storage
- High liquidity - trade during market hours
- Lower cost than physical gold
- No GST on purchase
- Can be used as collateral for loans

### Tax Treatment
- Held < 3 years: Short-term capital gains (as per income slab)
- Held > 3 years: Long-term capital gains (20% with indexation)

## 3. Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGB)

### Features
- Issued by Reserve Bank of India
- Denomination: 1 gram of gold
- Tenure: 8 years (exit option after 5 years)
- Interest: 2.50% per annum (paid semi-annually)
- Issue price: Based on average gold price

### Current Series (July 2026)
- Issue Price: ₹6,350 per gram
- Minimum: 1 gram
- Maximum: 4 kg per person per fiscal year
- Discount for online application: ₹50 per gram

### Benefits
- Interest income of 2.50% p.a.
- No storage concerns
- Can be used as collateral
- Capital gains tax exempt on maturity
- Tradeable on exchanges after lock-in

### Tax Benefits
- Interest: Taxable as per income tax slab
- Capital gains on maturity: Tax exempt
- Capital gains on premature sale: As per income tax rules

### Eligibility
- Resident Indians
- HUF, Trusts, Universities, Charitable Institutions

## 4. Gold Savings Account

### Features
- Save in grams of gold instead of rupees
- Minimum deposit: 0.1 gram
- Interest paid in gold: 1% p.a. (credited monthly)
- Withdraw in gold or cash equivalent

### Benefits
- Protection against gold price rise
- Earn interest in gold
- Flexible withdrawals
- No storage hassle

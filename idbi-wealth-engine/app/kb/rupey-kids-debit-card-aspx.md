# About The Card

* Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 2000/- and purchase at Point of Sale (POS) limit of Rs. 2000/- in a day.
* 1 loyalty point for every Rs. 100 spent on the Debit Card

##### Features

Daily Limits

You can withdraw cash up to Rs. 2,000/- and make purchases worth Rs. 2,000/- in a day

Insurance Cover\*

Customers are covered with an insurance cover upto Rs. 8000 for lost and counterfeit cards.

\*Effective 1st July 2013, for Insurance claims to be accepted and processed; there should be a minimum of 2 purchase transactions using the Debit Card in last 3 months prior to the event date.

Loyalty Redemptions#

The customer stands to earn 1 loyalty points for every Rs. 100 spent using the KIDS Card for purchases at merchant establishments.

*#Loyalty point reward structure as well as reward of loyalty points for different merchant categories is subject to revision from time to time.*

Discounts at Merchant Establishments

IDBI Bank regularly comes up with attractive offers and discounts at various merchant establishments through which KIDS Card customers can avail fabulous offers on the Card usage.

Domestic Validity

The IDBI Bank KIDS Card is valid only on India

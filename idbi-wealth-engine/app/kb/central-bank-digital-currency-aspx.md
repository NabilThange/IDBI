##### IDBI Bank Central Bank Digital Currency

Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC) is the legal tender issued by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) in a digital form, represented as Digital Rupee or e₹. It is the digital form of India’s physical currency, the Rupee (₹), and is fully interchangeable with existing currency at par (1:1). Just like traditional banknotes, CBDC is accepted as a medium of payment and legal tender. Its digital nature makes transactions faster, simpler, and more secure. It combines the trust of physical currency with the convenience and efficiency of digital payments, offering all the benefits of modern digital money.

**Primary Goals:**

* Reduce cash-handling costs and enhance payment transparency.
* Broaden financial inclusion especially in offline and remote zones.
* Enable programmable use cases: subsidies, travel allowances, geo-locked payments.
* Improve settlement speed and cross-border efficiency.

##### IDBI eRupee Mobile App

For retail usage, the Bank has developed a digital wallet based **“eRupee”** mobile application. Using this app, user can transact with any CBDC user or merchant through mobile numbers and QR codes.

Currently user can load money into wallet, sent it to beneficiary via mobile number and QR, collect money from beneficiary via QR and Redeem balance in to linked account.

Additionally, with UPI interoperability, users can make payments in digital rupee (CBDC) to any UPI-enabled individual or merchant by scanning their QR codes.

# Getting started with IDBI eRupee (CBDC App)

1. **Step 1:** Download IDBI eRupee App by scanning QR available on website or from Apple App Store/ Android Play Store
2. **Step 2:** Install app and start registration by Selecting the SIM linked to your IDBI Bank Account
3. **Step 3:** Follow the onscreen instruction and setup app login (PIN/Pattern/Biometric) followed by 6 digit CBDC Transaction PIN.
4. **Step 4:** Link eligible IDBI Bank account (Savings, Current-Proprietor) with active and linked debit card.
5. **Step 5:** Your Registration is completed, now you can load wallet and explore the digital currency of India in your mobile.

##### FAQs

What is CBDC or Digital Rupee (e₹)?

Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC) is a digital form of India’s physical currency issued by RBI. It is legal tender and can be used for transactions, just like any physical ₹ note.

Is Digital Rupee the same as cryptocurrency?

No. CBDC is backed by RBI and has intrinsic value, unlike cryptocurrencies which are decentralized and volatile.

What is an eRupee (e₹) wallet?

A digital wallet provided by IDBI Bank to store and transact CBDC (Digital Rupee).

What is the requirement for registration of IDBI eRupee wallet?

Eligible Savings/current account having active linked debit card and Bank registered mobile number required to activate eRupee wallet. Wallet Registration to be done via SIM data only.

How to use and transact with CBDC?

With IDBI eRupee app user can transact with CBDC. Customers maintaining eligible account with IDBI Bank can register the digital rupee wallet app. This “eRupee” wallet will be like your physical wallet in digital form on your Android/iOS device.

How can I transact if I do not have the required denomination or change?

Management of denominations is taken care by change management system of CBDC and the transaction will be processed, if otherwise in order. You can directly enter the amount in the enter amount filed to make payment.

Can CBDC be used for existing UPI merchant payments?

Yes, QR codes support both CBDC and UPI payments.

What if user enter wrong Wallet PIN during a transaction?

In case you input the wrong PIN more than 3 times a day, then transaction on IDBI eRupee app will be temporarily blocked. Customers need to reset the Wallet PIN.

What happens if device with wallet is lost or I change device?

Wallet can be restored using registered SIM and credentials. And you can recover your wallet using the same phone number/SIM on IDBI eRupee App.

For any dispute and emergency whom to contact.

In case of emergencies customers can instantly block their wallets by simply sending an SMS BLOCKCBDC on 7799437634 to Bank. For more details customer can contact Bank customer care on [customercare@idbi.co.in](mailto:customercare@idbi.co.in) and **1800-209-4324**

What is Auto Load feature and how does it work?

When you try to “Send Digital Rupee” and the amount is higher than your current wallet balance, Auto Load automatically debits the remaining required amount from your linked bank account.  
For example: If your IDBI eRupee wallet balance is ₹80 and you need to send ₹100 (via mobile number or QR scan), then: ₹80 will be used from your wallet, and the remaining ₹20 will be auto‑debited from your linked bank account. This ensures your payment/transfer is completed smoothly without interruption.

What is Pinless feature and how does it work?

The PIN-less feature allows you to make small-value payments up to ₹500 without entering your PIN, making everyday transactions faster and more convenient. With this feature, you can perform up to 10 transactions per day, with a total daily limit of ₹2,000.

How to enable PINLESS feature in app.

Navigate to User Profile> May wallet> Provide consent and Enable PINLESS Transaction

Can I use CBDC app to make payment on E-Com merchants apps?

With this feature that allows a merchant’s app to initiate a payment request directly on your IDBI eRupee application making payments faster, safer, and more convenient.

What is P-CBDC?

Programmable Central Bank Digital Currency (PCBDC) is a special, rule based version of the Digital Rupee (e₹) introduced by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). It allows money to be “programmed” so it can be used only for specific purposes, in specific locations, or within a defined time period.

Is my eRupee app enabled to receive Programmable Currency?

Yes, your eRupee app is fully compatible to receive Programmable Currency / CBDC issued by the Government or Corporate entities under any DBT or subsidy scheme.

IDBI Bank Ltd.

   Process Flow for NRI Online Account Opening

                                               Version: 1.02 dated 02nd April 2019

NRI customers can apply for a new NRI SB (NRE/NRO) account online. The customer
can apply for NRI SB Account with any of the RBG Branches of IDBI Bank by visiting
the link https://nri.idbibank.co.in/instaonline/ .

The Salient features of the Online application are as follows:

       NRIs can apply for both NRE and NRO account.
       NRIs can apply for NRE and NRO account with any of the RBG branches of
        IDBI Bank.
       NRIs can apply for NRI SB account with a co-applicant
       The customer shall be furnished with an Application Reference number on
        submitting the application.
       NRI customers residing in FATF countries (Annexure I) can additionally upload
        the scan copies of the self-attested documents in the link provided to their
        registered email ID upon successful submission of the online application.
       The Account Number and Customer ID shall be generated & communicated
        by the Bank to the customer within 7 working days in case of Online Upload
        of the requisite documents by the customer. In all other cases the same shall
        be generated & communicated by the Bank after receipt of the physical
        copy of the completed Account opening Form & KYC Documents sent by
        the customer to the mentioned address.

Process Flow:

       Customer       would      apply        for   NRI   SB   account   online   by   visiting
        https://nri.idbibank.co.in/instaonline/ .

       NRI customer would have to fill all his details and co-applicant details (if any)
        online and submit the same.

       On submission of the online form, a system generated acknowledgement e-
        mail having the Application Reference number, Branch Address, Schedule of
        facilities and a link through which the customer can download the pre-filled

        NRI account opening form (NRF) / Upload the scan copy of the complete
        account opening form & Self-attested copies of KYC documents (for FATF
        resident NRI customers) shall be sent on the customers registered e-mail Id.

       Customer would then be required to download the pre-filled NRF
        (https://nri.idbibank.co.in/instaonline/)    by    furnishing   the   1st applicant’s
        Passport Number and Date of Birth. Customer would have to affix his photo &
        signatures at all relevant pages. The scan copy of the Account opening Form
        (NRF) and KYC documents (Annexure II) would then have to be uploaded
        using      the      link     provided   in   the      acknowledgement          email
        (https://nri.idbibank.co.in/instaonlineFU/). On accessing the link an option for
        generating OTP will come. Two OTP’s one each on the registered mobile
        number (SMS OTP) & email ID (email OTP) will be sent. The SMS OTP & email
        OTP have to be entered in sequence and without spaces to login.

        mentioned in the system generated acknowledgement e-mail. KYC
        documents / Signature & Photographs have to be attested either by Embassy

       The NRI account opened on the basis of scanned copies of Account
        opening Form (NRF) / self-attested copies of KYC documents will remain in
        Debit freeze till the receipt of the physical copy of the complete set of
        documents (NRF, KYC documents, Cheque etc.). Customers are advised to
        courier the same post getting their account number/customer id details to
        the Branch address mentioned in the Welcome Email.

                                               Annexure II

        (KYC Documents required to open NRI accounts in case of
                               FATF Resident NRI Customers
    and desirous of availing the self-attested Online Upload facility)

    1. Passport Copy (front and back)
    2. Visa / Residency Card copy
    3. Initial account opening cheque from clients’ existing NRE/NRO/ Resident savings
        bank account in India, as applicable.
    4. Original bank statement of the clients’ overseas bank account with seal of the
        bank with signature. (The statement should not be more than 3 months old)
    5. Self-attested photocopy/original of any one of the latest overseas utility bill such
        as:
              a) Telephone
              b) Electricity
              c) Gas
              d) Water

        (The above documents should not be more than 3 months old)

    6. Self-attested Photocopy of any two of the following valid photo identity
        documents with signature such as:
              a) Pan Card issued in India
              b) Photo Credit Card issued overseas
              c) Overseas Driving license
              d) Overseas University/College Id card
              e) Overseas Health Card
              f) Overseas Civil Id / Resident permit
              g) Any other Photo Id document issued overseas by Govt. Authority of the
                 respective Country.

                                           Annexure III

                KYC Documents required to open NRI accounts

** (Documents to be attested by officials of Indian Embassy / High Commission /
         Consulate OR Public Notary OR officials of any Bank in the country of
                                               Residence).

    1. Photocopy of the Passport pages with:
         a) Name and address
         b) Specimen-signature
         c) Photograph
         d) Passport expiry date

    2. Copy of valid Visa / Work / Residence Permit (the visa could be either in
         the passport or given separately)

    3. PAN Card / Form 60

    4. Proof of Mailing Address (local or foreign address as selected on the
         accounting opening form)

    5. Latest photograph (passport size)

    6. Customer Profile for each applicant & Schedule of Facilities

    7.   Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA) declaration / CKYC form

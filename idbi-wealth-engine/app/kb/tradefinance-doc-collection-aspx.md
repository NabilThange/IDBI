## Collections | IDBI Bank Collections

##### Export Collections / Negotiations

We undertake export collections and negotiations of documents under letter of credit.
With our dedicated customer service the turnaround time is greatly reduced, thereby improving your cash flow.

##### Import Collections

All your import collections including documents received directly by you can be speedily
and efficiently handled by us and payments can be effected through our vast correspondent network.

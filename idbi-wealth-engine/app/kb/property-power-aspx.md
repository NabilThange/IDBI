# Property Power

# Overview

The Product aims to cater to the needs of MSME segment by way of providing loan against commercial and residential properties owned by them. The end use of the loan would be for various business related purposes or to take care of any financial requirements viz., retiring of high cost debt, balance transfer/ takeover or any other business exigencies.

# Features

Eligible Segments

All potential and existing MSME customers of the Bank engaged in the business for the last 2 years.

Facility

Term Loan/ Overdraft limit to fund business needs (excluding for speculative purposes)

Loan Amount

Minimum: Above Rs. 10 lakh  
Maximum: Rs. 500 lakh

Tenor

Term Loan: Max 7 years  
OD: Max 12 months (Annual renewal)

Pricing

Linked to banks Repo Linked Lending Rate (RLLR) and Rating

Security

Equitable Mortgage or Registered Mortgage of Residential / Commercial property

Guarantee

Personal guarantee of the borrower

Processing Charges

As per Extant applicable Schedule of Charges.

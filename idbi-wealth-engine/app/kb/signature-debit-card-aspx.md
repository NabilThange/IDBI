# VISA Signature PayWave Debit Card

# About The Card

* Cash withdrawal limit of RS. 3 lakh and purchase limit at Point of Sale (POS) of RS. 5 lakh in a day.
* 3 loyalty points for every RS. 100 spent at Merchant locations.
* 4 Free visits per calendar quarter at participating Airport lounges in India.
* Simply wave/tap the card on the contactless Point of Sale (PoS) and pay without entering PIN for purchases upto Rs. 5000.

##### Features

Free Airport Lounge Access

IDBI Bank VISA Signature PayWave Debit Card offers 4 free visits per calendar quarter at participating Airport lounge in India. For List of participating Lounge please visit <https://www.visa.co.in/content/dam/VCOM/regional/ap/india/global-elements/documents/in-participating-airport-lounges.pdf>

High Withdrawal & transaction limit

Cash withdrawal limit of RS. 3 lakh and purchase limit at Point of Sale (POS) of RS. 5 lakh in a day

Online transactions

You can use your IDBI Bank Signature Debit Card on the Internet for buying travel or movie tickets, paying your bills and much more.
All you need to do is register yourself for “Verified by VISA".

Accelerated Loyalty Points on Usage at POS#

IDBI Bank Signature Debit Cardholder will get 3 loyalty points for every 100 spent at Merchant locations.

*#Loyalty point reward structure as well as reward of loyalty points for different merchant categories is subject to
revision from time to time*

Enhanced Insurance Cover\*

Air Accident Insurance Cover- 25 lakhs
  
Personal Accident cover – 10 Lakh
  
Loss of checked baggage – 2 lakhs
for 90 days  
Purchase protection – Rs.2,00,000/- for 90 days.
  
Fire and burglary for household contents – 2 lakhs

*\*Effective 1st July 2013, for Insurance claims to be accepted and processed; there should be a minimum of 2 purchase transactions using the Debit Card in last 3 months prior to the event date.*

Global Customer Assistance Service

The Signature Debit Card enables you to access the Global Customer assistance Service
anytime from anywhere across the world. This service is offered by VISA and can be availed for following:

* Lost/Stolen Card reporting
* Emergency Card Replacement/ Cash disbursement
* Emergency inquiries
* Miscellaneous inquiries

Exclusive Offers

The Card offers you the exclusive offers and discounts over a range of categories like: Dining, Travel, Lifestyle,
Shopping and Health & Fitness. The Signature Debit Card also brings you a lot of supplementary benefits like:

* Concierge Online
* Travel Assistance
* Car rental & Limousine service
* Sports & entertainment service
* Pre-trip assistance service.
* Business & Services assistance

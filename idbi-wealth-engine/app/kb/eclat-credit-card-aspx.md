##### Features and Benefits

Earn More while you Spend

The Co-branded Eclat Select Credit Card understands your lifestyle and rewards you for your needs. You will earn 4 Delight Points for every Rs. 100 spent by you using your Eclat Credit Card. So enjoy extra rewards on travelling, shopping, dine-out or watching movies! Get rewarded for all your spends! Additionally, enjoy bonus delight points as following:

* 500 bonus delight points on card activation.
* 1,000 bonus delight points on first transaction of Rs. 5,000 or more within 30 days of card activation.
* Additional milestone benefit of 1,500 points on net annual spends of Rs. 2 lakhs.
* Additional milestone benefit of 2,000 points on net annual spends of Rs. 4 lakhs.

**\*Delight points would be accrued only on transactions except the ones exempted from delight points accrual. Refer the exempted transaction categories [here.](https://www.idbidelight.com/content/faq)**

Interest Free Credit

Enjoy Interest free credit of up to 48 days\* on your purchases and manage your payments as per your convenience. You can choose to pay the total outstanding amount, minimum amount due or any other amount higher than the minimum amount due.
\*Refer MITC for more details

Drive On

Waiver of 1%\* fuel surcharge every time you fill fuel using Eclat Select Card across all fuel stations for transactions in the range of Rs. 400 to Rs. 5000. So keep driving and enjoying with friends and family.
  
\*Maximum waiver of upto Rs. 500 per month

Insurance Covers

Accidental Death and Permanent Disability: Cover of Rs. 10.00 lakh in case of loss of life or permanent disability due to accident.
Credit Shield Cover: Outstanding Dues as on the date of death.

Criteria:Age -18 years to 70 years
Card Usage - One successful financial or non-financial transaction at any channel is done in the last 90 days prior to the incident.

Airport Lounge Access

Enjoy luxury at select airport lounges in India. No matter which airline or class you fly, your Eclat Select card offers you well deserved rest and use of facilities. You will surely find it useful to relieve travel fatigue and escape airport chaos.
  
  
The airport lounge access is being provided by RuPay and is subject to change as per RuPay’s discretion from time to time without any prior notice.
  
  
For the list of participating Lounges please
[click here](https://www.rupay.co.in/lounges)

**Note:** Per quarter period would be considered from 15th of the month i.e. Jan 15 - Apr 15, Apr 15 - Jul 15, Jul 15 - Oct 15 and Oct 15 - Jan 15.

Wider Acceptance

Get more freedom with your Eclat Select Credit Card. You can use it at over 5.81 million merchants in India and over 44 million merchants abroad.

Zero Lost Card Liability

In case of loss of credit card, report it immediately at our 24-Hour Credit Card Customer Care 1800-209-4324 (Toll Free) or 022-67719100 (Non-toll free). The card will be immediately blocked for all transactions to prevent card misuse. Any fraudulent transaction after reporting the loss will be covered by the Bank.

Family Cards

You may apply for upto 2 add-on cards for your family members.

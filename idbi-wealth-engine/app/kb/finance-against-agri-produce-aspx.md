[Apply Now](apply-now.aspx)

# Overview

The bank is financing to farmers through various post-harvest funding products in order to
avoid distress sale immediately after the harvest. Funding to farmers against hypothecation
of agri produce is a variant of post-harvest funding in which farmers can avail the finance
against agri produce in a hassle free manner. It ensures post-harvest funding for major agri
produces and improves better price realization, besides ensuring availability of quality
produce round the year.

# Who is eligible for the Loan?

Individual farmers.

# Loan :

CC limit - Minimum Rs.5 lakh and Maximum – Rs. 50 Lakh

# Tenor

1 year.

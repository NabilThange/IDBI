### Treasury - Long-Term Foreign Exchange (LTFX)

[Corporate
Service Charges](corp-service-charges.aspx)

##### Overview

Long-term foreign exchange is nothing but a USD/INR, other FC/INR and Cross currency forward rate
quoted, for periods beyond one year. The outright forward market for the USD/INR quotes forward
points until one year only. Hence LTFX is offered as a product to subvert this constraint and
enable customer sell/buy forward USD/INR beyond one year.

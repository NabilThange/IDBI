##### Payment Options

Auto-Debit Facility

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Auto Debit payment option saves you from the effort of remembering your payment due date every month towards the payment of your IDBI Bank Credit Card dues. You need to authorize IDBI Bank to deduct the amount directly from your IDBI Bank savings/current account every month and your Card Account will be credited.

Net banking

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Using your IDBI net banking facility, make payment towards credit card dues via direct debit from your IDBI Bank Savings/Current Account.

NEFT

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Pay your IDBI Bank Credit Card dues through NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer). It's a convenient Payment option by which you can use any bank account that offers NEFT facility to pay your IDBI Bank Credit Card bill online. Use IFSC code "IBKL0NEFT01" and add Bank as "IDBI Bank Ltd." and credit card number as beneficiary account number.

Cheque/ Draft

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Drop the Cheque/Draft into any of the IDBI Bank drop boxes placed in the IDBI Bank branches and ATMs. The Cheque/Draft should be made payable to IDBI Bank Card Number xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx of name as embossed on the card.

Cash Payment

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Walk-in to any IDBI Bank branch and pay their credit card dues by mentioning their card number, amount and the contact number. An instant receipt shall be provided to the customer.

Visa Money Transfer

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Pay your IDBI Bank Visa Credit Card dues using Visa Money Transfer facility through other bank account.

IPay

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

You can conveniently pay from different bank accounts directly to your card account.

**Note:**

A) Cardholders are advised to make the payment well in advance of the Payment Due Date to ensure that the payment credit reflects on the Payment Due Date.

B) Payments made to the cardholders account will be settled in the following order -  
1) Interest 2) Tax 3) Fee 4) Cash 5) EMI 6) Purchase(s)

What is a Chip Card?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

A chip Credit Card is a Card with a microchip embedded in it. When a Chip Credit Card is used at a chip enabled terminal at a merchant outlet or at an ATM, the payment system reads information from the Card chip to establish the Card details. The chip offers a more secure payment mechanism compared to the traditional Magnetic Stripe, and reduces the probability of skimming, i.e., copying of personal data from the information stored on the Card. A Euro pay MasterCard Visa (EMV) Chip + PIN Card mandates the use of a Personal Identification Number (PIN) to authorize purchase transactions providing an extra layer of security. The PIN for your Card is dispatched to you in a separate packet than the Card and should be known only to you. We advise you to not share your Card and your Card PIN with anyone.

How should an EMV Chip + PIN Card be used?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Step 1: Ensure the Merchant Inserts (dips) the Card inside the chip terminal instead of swiping the Card.

Step 2: Follow machine instructions to select type of payment (Credit/Debit) and amount.

Step 3: Confirm the amount and enter 4-digit Card PIN to authorize the transaction.

Step 4: Remove the Card when the transaction is complete.

Would every merchant terminal ask for a PIN?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Merchants in India are progressively moving towards Chip + PIN configuration on their terminals. If a merchant POS terminal (domestic or international) is enabed for PIN authentication, a PIN would be mandatorily required for transactions on the EMV Chip + PIN Card. If the merchant terminal is not yet configured, Card members would still be able to transact by just signing a charge slip, generated on using the new Chip + PIN Card.

Is it safe to give merchants my PIN details?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

No, you should never share your Card PIN with anyone, including the merchant. You should always insist on entering the PIN yourself.

What is Revolving credit facility?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

**When you receive your statement, you have the flexibility of selecting from any of the following options**

Pay the total amount due.

Pay only the minimum amount due.

Pay any amount ranging from the minimum amount due to the total amount due.

Should you opt for either of the last two options, then the unpaid amount due is carried forward to the next billing cycle. This is referred to as the revolving credit facility. Interest will be charged on the revolving portion of the payment due as per fees and charges mentioned in MITC.

When do I start paying interest on new purchases if I am already revolving credit?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

If you are on revolving credit, any fresh purchases attract interest from their respective dates of purchase.

When will the interest charge stop?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

If all outstanding amount is paid, the interest charges will cease to apply immediately.

What happens if I pay more than the total amount due?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

The excess amount shows up in your monthly statement and is adjusted against future purchases.

Can I retain my credit card if I emigrate?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Credit cards (including International cards) are issued to resident Indians only. Therefore, under RBI guidelines, you have to clear all your outstanding and surrender your credit card to the issuing bank if you are proceeding abroad on long term employment or on emigration.

What is Minimum Amount Due (MAD)?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Credit cards provide you the option to pay the minimum amount due on the bill due date and revolve the rest of the outstanding amount. The Minimum Amount Due (MAD) is calculated as:
MAD = 5% of Purchase and Cash Withdrawal + 100% of Joining Fees, Cash Withdrawal Fee, Interest on cash withdrawal, GST & GST on Interest

What is the EMI facility on IDBI Bank Credit Card?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

EMI facility provides you the option to repay the outstanding amount in equated monthly installments. The minimum EMI convertible amount is 3000 for IDBI Bank Credit Card. You can opt to pay the amount over 3, 6, 9,12, 18, 24, 30 or 36 months at nominal interest rate as applicable.

Please note that this facility is available only after 6 months of issuance of Credit Card.

What if I cross my credit limit?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

If the transaction is approved, an over-limit charge would be levied to the card account. The over-limit charge is 2.5% of the amount by which the credit limit is exceeded (subject to a minimum charge of Rs. 500).

What is the insurance cover on the IDBI Bank Credit Card?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

On the IDBI Bank Aspire & Imperium Platinum Cards the insurance cover is for Zero Loss Liability after reporting lost/stolen card or any fraudulent transaction on card. The Credit Cardholder is covered for up to the card limit on fraudulent transactions on lost/stolen card when reported within 48 Hrs. of the transaction. Insurance benefit is provided directly to Credit Card holders by third party Insurance Company. However, you will be liable for all charges incurred on your credit card before you report the loss of your credit card.

On the IDBI Bank Royale Signature & Euphoria World Credit Cards, the insurance cover is for Air Accidental Death of up to 25 lakhs and covers Zero Loss Liability after reporting lost/stolen card or any fraudulent transaction on card.

The Credit Cardholder is covered for up to 5,00,000 on fraudulent transactions on lost/stolen card when reported within 48 Hrs. of the transaction. Insurance benefit provided directly to Credit Card holders by third party Insurance Company. However, you will be liable for all charges incurred on your credit card before you report the loss of your credit

NOTE: The settlement of the claim registered by you will be subject to approval of the claim after independent investigations by Insurance Company and shall be limited to the eligible insurance amount as per your card type as mentioned.

What does the Zero Lost card Liability Insurance cover include?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

All fraudulent use of lost or stolen credit cards including due to counterfeit / lost in transit / ATM transactions / online transactions/ point of sale merchant establishments transactions are covered under zero loss liability cover. Once the card is reported as lost or stolen it will be immediately hot listed/blocked to avoid any misuse of the card. The customer has zero lost card liability for any misuse after card has been hot listed/blocked.

The period of reporting lost/stolen card is within 48 hours of card loss and within 180 days from the date of receipt of statement where in the alleged online fraud transaction is reflected.

What do I have to do, in case my credit card is lost or stolen?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

In case your card is lost or stolen immediately call Customer Care on 1800-209-4324 (Toll Free) or 022-67719100 (Call Charges Applicable) and get your card deactivated / blocked to prevent misuse.

What do I have to do to claim insurance if my credit card is lost or stolen and misused?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

a. Please submit the following mandatory documents to the IDBI Bank Branch within 7 days from day of event / loss to register your claim-

* Submit application giving details of the transaction and the incident
* Copy of FIR/Letter to Police Authorities

b. Bank will send the claim request to Insurance Company and Insurance Company will process the claims as per their internal policy.

NOTE: The settlement of the claim registered by you will be subject to approval of the claim after independent investigations by third party Insurance Company and shall be limited to the eligible insurance amount as per your card type as mentioned.

What happens if the claim is approved by the Insurance Company?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

If the insurance claim is approved by the Insurance Company the disputed transaction amount[s] would be credited to your card account, subject to the eligible insurance amount. In case the disputed amount exceeds the eligible claim amount you shall be liable to pay for the excess amount of the disputed transaction(s) as reflecting in your IDBI Bank Credit Card Statement.

What happens if the claim gets rejected or I fail to register a claim with the Insurance Company?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

In case you fail to register your claim with the Insurance Company or if your claim is rejected by the Insurance Company you will be liable to pay the amount of the disputed transaction(s) as reflecting in your IDBI Bank Credit Card Statement.

What is Air Travel Accident Insurance Cover?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

In the unfortunate event of loss of life due to an air travel accident, your nominee will receive the benefits of accident insurance. However the claim must be made within 1 year of the death of the card holder. IDBI Bank Credit Card provides complimentary Air Travel Accident Insurance Cover of up to 25 lakhs for all IDBI Bank Royale Signature & Euphoria World Credit Card customers through tie-up with third party Insurance Company.

Please ensure that the details of the nominee are provided when the credit card application form is being filled by you. If not already done, fill up the form and submit to nearest IDBI Bank Branch.

Who is eligible to claim the Air Travel Accident Insurance Cover?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

All IDBI Bank Royale Signature & Euphoria World Credit Card customer's nominee is eligible to claim the air travel accident cover. However Air travel accident is valid only if the IDBI Bank Royale Signature/Euphoria World Credit Card has been used for a minimum of two transactions / activities on any ATM/POS/e-commerce terminal in last 3 months preceding the incident.

How to Claim for Air Travel Accident Insurance Cover?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Nominee has to report Death of the Cardholder by giving written application along with the proof of death and relationship with the cardholder and submit it to the nearest IDBI Bank branch.

Bank will check all documents and forward the same to the Insurance Company

If the claim is approved, Insurance Company will transfer the funds to the Bank. The Bank would then credit the funds in the Nominee's Account.

How can I earn Delight Points? How many Points do I earn per transaction?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

Whenever you use your Credit Card for any purchase\* you will earn Delight Points. The Delight Points structure for each card is given below

| Royale Signature Credit Card | 3 Delight Points for every 100 spent |

|

How can I know the Delight Points balance?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

You can check the Delight Points on your card from

* Your latest Credit Card statement or
* You can log in to IDBI Bank Credit Card Net-Banking and check the details.

Do Delight Points accrued on my card expire?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

All Delight Points accrued on your IDBI Bank Credit Card expires after 3 years from the date of issuance of points.

How to redeem the Delight Points?

![](assets/images/icons/up-arrow-green-small.svg)

You can redeem your Delight Points and get cashback for the value of Delight Points. The conversion rate is every 1 Delight Points=0.25 paisa. The cashback will be credited to your credit card account and can be adjusted against your credit card payment outstanding. A minimum of 1000 Delight Points are required for redemption.

**Process of Redemption**

Customer can request for redemption by logging onto his IDBI Bank Credit Card Net-Banking account or visit- [www.idbidelight.idbi.bank.in](https://www.idbidelight.idbi.bank.in/)

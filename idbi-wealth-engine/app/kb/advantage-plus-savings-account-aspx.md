##### IDBI Bank Advantage Savings Plus Account

The Advantage Savings Plus Account provides you a complete banking convenience and financial package to access your money with the ease. IDBI Bank offers you a range of savings account for optimal management of your money. With Account, you not only save your money but also make it grow.

# Features and Benefits

* Rs. 25,000 Minimum Average Balance requirements across all locations.
* International Debit cum ATM Card
* 25% discount on locker rent for primary holder
* POS limit through Rupay Platinum card of Rs. 1,00,000/- per day

* Free eight ATM transactions at other Bank ATMs at all Locations
* Pass-book facility
* Two zero balance accounts for kids
* ATM cash withdrawal limit of Rs.75,000/- per day

##### FAQs

What is the minimum balance that I need to maintain in the Advantage Savings Plus Account?

You need to maintain an Monthly Average Balance (MAB) of Rs. 25,000 across all locations.

What is the rate of interest applicable on the Advantage Savings Plus Account?

Interest on Savings Bank Account is applicable as per Banks latest prevailing rate of Interest hosted on Banks website.

When is interest credited to the Advantage Savings Plus Account?

Sb interest is credited quarterly.

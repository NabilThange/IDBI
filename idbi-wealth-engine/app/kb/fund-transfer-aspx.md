# Fund Transfer

You can use Internet Banking, ATMs or our branches for transferring money across the country. The only information you need to know is the 16-digit card number of the transferee. To carry out a transfer, simply log on to IDBI Bank's Internet Banking at www.idbibank.in, or visit the nearest IDBI Bank ATM or branch and transfer the required amount. The money is directly credited into the recipient’s card, no matter where he is.

[Click here](faq.aspx) for a list of frequently asked questions or click on 'login' to transfer funds now.

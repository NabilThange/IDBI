# Benefits of Royal Account

###### International Signature Debit Card

Can be used in India and abroad to make purchases at Merchant locations and
withdraw local currency at ATMs.

###### Petrol Surcharge Waiver

Surcharge will be waived only for Platinum Card transactions with a value between
Rs. 400 and Rs. 5,000.

###### Daily Limits

Customers can withdraw cash up to Rs. 1,50,000 and make purchases worth Rs.
3,00,000 in a day.

###### Online Transactions

Can be used online, for shopping, booking air / rail / movie tickets & utility
bill payments.

###### Loyalty Redemptions

2 points earned for every Rs. 100 spent. Redeem each point for cash credit of
Rs. 1 to the account for every point.

###### Personalized Services

Dedicated Relationship Manager for Investment advisory, Portfolio management and
personalized services.

###### Royale Account

Rs. 3 lakhs Average Monthly balance in savings account or Rs. 15 lakhs Fixed
Deposit

###### Royale Plus Account

Rs. 5 lakhs Average Monthly balance in savings account or Rs. 25 lakhs Fixed
Deposit

##### Features and Benefits

Earn More while you Spend

The `Winnings' Credit Card always gives you something extra. You will earn 2 Delight Points for every Rs. 100 spent on your card. So shop till you drop with your `Winnings` Credit Card and get irresistible offers from globally renowned brands redeemable against your Delight Points. Additionally, enjoy bonus delight points as following:

* 500 bonus delight points on card activation.
* 500 bonus delight points on first transaction of Rs. 1,000 or more within 30 days of card activation.
* Additional milestone benefit of 1,000 points on net annual spends of Rs. 1 lakhs.
* Additional milestone benefit of 1,500 points on net annual spends of Rs. 2 lakhs.

**\*Delight points would be accrued only on transactions except the ones exempted from delight points accrual. Refer the exempted transaction categories [here.](https://www.idbidelight.com/content/faq)**

Interest Free Credit

Enjoy interest free credit of up to 48 days\* on your purchases and manage your payments as per your convenience. You can choose to pay the total outstanding amount, minimum amount due or any other amount higher than the minimum amount due
  
  
\*Refer MITC for more details

Drive On

Waiver of 1%\* fuel surcharge every time you fill fuel using IDBI Bank Winnings RuPay Select card across all fuel stations for
transactions in the range of Rs. 400 to Rs. 4000. So keep driving and enjoying with friends and family.
  
  
\*Maximum waiver of upto Rs. 400 per month

Personal Accident Insurance Cover

Accidental Death: Cover of Rs. 10.00 lakh in case of loss of life due to accident.  
Permanent Disability: Cover of Rs. 10.00 lakh in case of permanent disability due to accident.  
Criteria:  
Age -18 years to 70 years  
Card Usage - One successful financial or non-financial transaction at any channel is done in the last 90 days prior to the incident.

Airport Lounge Access

A smart man always makes the smartest move. Get away from the terminal traffic during a delayed flight to immerse in the comfort of plush airport lounges with delicious cuisine in India or abroad, absolutely free with your IDBI Bank `Winnings' Credit Card.

The airport lounge access is being provided by RuPay and is subject to change as per RuPay’s discretion from time to time without any prior notice.

IDBI Bank Winnings Credit Card offers 2 complimentary lounge visits per calendar quarter at participating Airport Lounges in India and 2 complimentary lounge visits per calendar half year at participating Airport Lounges outside India.

For list of participating lounges, please [Click Here](https://www.rupay.co.in/lounges)

**Note:** Per quarter period would be considered from 15th of the month i.e. Jan 15 - Apr 15, Apr 15 - Jul 15, Jul 15 - Oct 15 and Oct 15 - Jan 15.

Wider Acceptance

You can use your Winnings Credit Card for booking Air/IRCTC/Bus/Hotels/ Movie tickets or enjoying Dining, Shopping, Paying Utility Bills, Recharge, Fuel and much more at over **39.3 million** merchants and **1.88 million** ATMS spread across India and abroad, securely with RuPay PaySecure and also earn Delight points on your transactions.

Zero Lost Card Liability

In case of loss of credit card, report it immediately at our 24-Hour Customer Care 1800-209-4324 (Toll free) / 022-67719100 (Non-toll free). The card will be immediately blocked for all transactions to prevent card misuse. Any fraudulent transaction after reporting the loss will be covered by the Bank.

Family Cards

Make IDBI Bank your family Bank by gifting your family members (only for members above 18 years) an add-on Winnings RuPay Credit Card. The add-on card enables your family members to avail all the benefits and features applicable on the primary card. The reward points earned on the add-on cards will be clubbed with those of the primary card and can be redeemed as per the higher eligibility.

You may apply for upto 2 add-on cards for your family members.

Eligibility

* Primary card holder should be in the age bracket of 21 years to 60 years (65 years for self-employed).
* Add-on card holder should be 18 years and above
* Should be a resident of India

The above eligibility criteria are only indicative.
The Bank reserves the right to approve or decline applications for Credit Card.

Documentation

* ID Proof: Voter ID/ Passport/ Driving License/ PAN card/ Adhaar Card
* Residence Proof: Electricity/ Telephone Bill/ Election Card/ Passport/ Adhaar Card
* Income Proof: Latest 3 months’ Salary Slip/ Latest 3 months Bank Statement/ Latest IT Return//Form 16
* Recent Photograph

The above list is only indicative and documents required may vary on a case to case basis.

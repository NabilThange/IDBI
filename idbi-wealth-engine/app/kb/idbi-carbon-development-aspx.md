# IDBI Carbon Developments

##### Overview

IDBI Bank also publishes a monthly newsletter called "IDBI Carbon Developments". The magazine covers the analysis
of the pricing of Certified Emission Reductions (CERs) on spot and futures, status of CDM project registratio
n and various other developments happening in the carbon market, Renewable Energy Certificate (REC) trading and
Perform Achieve and Trade (PAT) mechanism launched by the Govt. of India.

##### Code of Bank’s Commitment to Customers

IDBI Bank is committed to provide fair and transparent treatment to all its customers availing its banking services.

IDBI Bank is amongst the first banks to register with the Banking Codes and Standards Board of India to
voluntarily accept the Code of Bank's Commitment to Customers.

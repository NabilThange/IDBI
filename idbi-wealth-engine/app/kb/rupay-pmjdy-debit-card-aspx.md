# RuPay PMJDY Card

# About The Card

* Government of India's initiative towards affordable basic banking services.
* Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) is a National Mission for Financial Inclusion to ensure access to financial services, namely, Banking / Savings & Deposit Accounts, Remittance, Credit, Insurance, Pension in an affordable manner.
* RuPay PMJDY Debit Card is issued with accounts opened under Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana.

  

# Features

**Transaction Limits*** ATM Transaction: Rs. 10,000/-per day.
* PoS/Online Payments or Purchase : Rs. 10,000/-per day.
* Aggregate Transaction Limit : Rs. 10,000/-per day.

* The RuPay PMJDY Debit Cards can be used at all ATMs, POS terminals and e-commerce websites.
* Customers are covered with Personal Accident and Permanent total disability Insurance cover\* of Rs. 2 Lakhs.

\*The cover will be provided by NPCI and their standard T&C will apply.

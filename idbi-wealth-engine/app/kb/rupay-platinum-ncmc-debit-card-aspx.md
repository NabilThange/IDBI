# RuPay Platinum NCMC Card

# About The Card

* Cash withdrawal limit of Rs. 1 lakh and purchase limit at Point of Sale (POS) of Rs. 1 lakh in a day.
* 2 loyalty points for every Rs. 100 purchase.
* 2 free visits per calendar quarter at participating Airport Lounges.
* Simply wave/tap the card on the contactless Point of Sale (PoS) and pay without entering PIN for purchases upto Rs. 5000.
* NCMC Cards will support small value offline transactions till Rs. 500.
